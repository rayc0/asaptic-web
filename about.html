BUSINESS MODEL

Principal-reseller. Deposit-first economics. AI-operated pipeline.

Three structural properties that define how Asaptic operates — and why they matter to buyers evaluating a new sourcing counterparty.

PRINCIPAL-RESELLER

We are the commercial counterparty, not a middleman.

Asaptic takes title to goods and acts as the seller of record on every transaction. This means your contract is with Asaptic — not an unnamed factory. Liability, warranty, and compliance obligations sit with us, not passed through to an upstream party you have no visibility into. We source from verified factories and stand behind the product, including clinical device sourcing with full compliance documentation. This is a structurally different relationship from freight brokers, trading agencies, or commission-based sourcing agents.

Deposit-First Economics

30% proforma invoice reserves your production slot; balance is settled against bill of lading. Near-zero working capital exposure for Asaptic. This is the same payment mechanism used by large buying groups and is a standard feature of factory-direct trade — not a sign of counterparty risk. See our sourcing process for a step-by-step walkthrough.

AI-Operated Pipeline (~99% autonomous)

Quotation, supplier qualification research, compliance document assembly, and correspondence are handled by AI. This means faster quotes, more thorough documentation, and 24/7 responsiveness. Every outgoing commitment — purchase order confirmation, PI issuance, contract signature — receives human review before dispatch. Ready to start? Engage with us to open a sourcing inquiry.

SCOPE BOUNDARIES

What Asaptic does not do.

Clarity on scope saves time for both parties. If your requirement falls outside these boundaries, we will say so at first contact rather than after extended qualification.

B2B ONLY

No consumer goods. No retail. No direct-to-consumer fulfilment.

Asaptic operates exclusively in business-to-business supply chains. Our minimum engagement is a commercial purchase order with a qualified business buyer. We do not supply individuals, do not list on consumer marketplaces, and do not provide fulfilment services for retail inventory. If your buying entity is a registered business with a genuine procurement requirement, you are in scope.

No Unverified Factory Brokering

We do not pass buyer RFQs to an open pool of unvetted suppliers and take a commission on whatever responds. Every factory in our pipeline has been qualified through direct engagement, transaction history, or an on-the-ground audit process.

Not Raising Capital

Asaptic is not seeking investment, does not have an open funding round, and is not interested in unsolicited investor introductions. Our operating model is designed around trade economics, not venture capital.

Affiliated Ventures — Disclosed on Request

Asaptic does not name affiliated ventures or related operating entities publicly. Details are disclosed under a signed commercial engagement or NDA. Inquiries can be directed to engage@asaptic.com.
