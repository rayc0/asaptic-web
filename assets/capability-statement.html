Principal-reseller for certified medical devices and deep-tech components.

Asaptic operates a sourcing and compliance pipeline for regulated clinical devices, photonics, and industrial deep-tech components. The pipeline is approximately 99% AI-operated for intake, qualification, evidence assembly, and document drafting, with human review on every outgoing commercial or compliance commitment.

Asaptic is affiliated with established Asia-Pacific clinical-nutrition operations.

Core Capabilities

Clinical Device Sourcing

  • Health Canada Class II MDALL and TGA Class IIa ARTG certified factory pathways.
  • Bioimpedance/BIA and body-composition analyzer sourcing.
  • Canada and Australia hospital, aged-care, and distributor procurement support.

Photonics

  • TFLN wafers and electro-optic modulators.
  • Direct foundry access with qualification evidence review.
  • Five-business-day sample availability for selected SKUs.

Industrial Deep-Tech Components

  • Power, sensing, robotics, RF, and specialist component channels.
  • Supplier identity, export-control, and certification pre-screening.
  • Commercial document packs for buyer review and internal approval.
