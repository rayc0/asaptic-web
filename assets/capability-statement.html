Regulatory-filtered, factory-direct sourcing of certified clinical devices and deep-tech components for Western B2B buyers — principal-reseller, deposit-secured, full compliance documentation.

Asaptic works as the buyer's commercial counterparty for qualified cross-border supply: we filter factories and foundries for regulatory readiness, assemble evidence packages, issue the proforma invoice, and coordinate shipment documentation.

Core Capabilities

Clinical Devices

Certified body-composition device sourcing

  • Bioimpedance and body-composition analysers for Canada and Australia hospital, aged-care, distributor, and procurement teams.
  • Factories qualified for Health Canada Class II MDALL and/or TGA Class IIa ARTG registration, verified in the official government databases.
  • ISO 13485 required, with a compliance document package covering certificates, listings, declarations, labelling, and shipment evidence.
  • Demand context includes GLIM malnutrition and EWGSOP2 sarcopenia screening, plus AN-ACC and Quality Indicator unplanned-weight-loss reporting.
Photonics / Deep-Tech

China-foundry access for specialist components

  • TFLN thin-film lithium niobate wafers and electro-optic modulators with direct China-foundry access.
  • Five-business-day sample availability for standard specifications, subject to stock and final technical confirmation.
  • Foundry provenance documentation, specification traceability, export-control screening, and commercial document packs.
  • Suitable for Western B2B buyers that need qualified specialist supply without taking on unverified supplier or documentation risk.

How We Work

Spec briefBuyer provides product, quantity, destination, end use, and certificate requirements.
Factory qualificationAsaptic screens supplier identity, registrations, ISO 13485, export controls, and document readiness.
PI + 30% depositAsaptic issues the proforma as principal and counterparty; the deposit secures production.
Production + documentsFactory produces while Asaptic assembles the compliance and shipment document package.
Shipment + 70%Buyer pays the balance against bill of lading or agreed shipment evidence.

Deposit-first purchasing keeps buyer working-capital exposure near zero until the qualified supply path, evidence pack, and shipment basis are visible. Asaptic is not a broker, manufacturer, or licence holder; Asaptic is the principal-reseller and commercial counterparty.

Compliance & Verification

  • Clinical-device factories checked against MDALL at mdall-limh.hc-sc.gc.ca and ARTG at tga.gov.au/resources/artg.
  • ISO 13485 quality-management evidence required for applicable clinical-device factories.
  • Export-control screening applied to supplier, destination, end use, and relevant technical specifications.
  • Buyers can independently verify every certificate, database listing, and compliance document provided in the package.

Why Factory-Direct

Factory-direct sourcing gives Western B2B buyers a meaningful price advantage versus incumbent Western brands while preserving equivalent compliance documentation, traceability, and procurement-ready evidence.
