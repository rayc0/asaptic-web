Verticals

Clinical Devices

  • Clinical body-composition and bioimpedance devices for Canada and Australia hospital, aged-care, distributor, and procurement teams.
  • Factory verification against applicable Health Canada MDALL and Australian ARTG evidence where relevant.

Photonics / Deep-Tech

TFLN/LNOI component sourcing

  • TFLN and LNOI photonics wafers and modulators for Western laboratories and specialist technical buyers.
  • Supplier screening, provenance checks, specification traceability, and export-control review before order execution.
  • Commercial documentation assembled for technical procurement teams that need direct specialist supply.

The Model

  • Deposit-first procurement: Asaptic issues the proforma invoice as the commercial counterparty, then coordinates production and document collection.
  • Approximately zero working capital: the buyer deposit funds the qualified order path rather than requiring inventory speculation.
  • Full compliance docs: evidence packages are assembled before shipment and remain available for buyer review.
  • MDALL and ARTG factory verification: applicable clinical-device claims are checked against official registry evidence without publishing third-party licence numbers here.

Credibility

Asaptic positions itself as an independent sourcing partner with transparent registry verification, buyer-verifiable documentation, and a Hong Kong common-law base for cross-border commercial contracting.
