Reusable Disclaimer Blocks

Copy the appropriate block into your document, email, quote, or web page. Each block is self-contained and scoped. Do not mix blocks without legal review. Blocks may be combined where multiple contexts apply.

1. Website Footer — Full Version

Use: Website footer, company profile PDF

Asaptic (HK) Ltd is a principal-reseller and sourcing intermediary. Asaptic is not the manufacturer, regulatory licence holder, importer of record, or clinical advisor for any device listed on this site unless explicitly stated in writing for a specific product. Device regulatory clearances (including Health Canada MDALL and TGA ARTG registrations) are held by the respective device manufacturers or their appointed sponsors. Buyers are responsible for verifying current regulatory status through official government registers, confirming suitability for their intended clinical or commercial use, and ensuring compliance with all applicable import, licensing, and professional requirements in their jurisdiction. No content on this site constitutes medical, clinical, regulatory, or legal advice.

2. Website Footer — Short Version

Use: Page footers, product listing pages

Asaptic (HK) Ltd is a principal-reseller only. Asaptic is not the manufacturer, regulatory licence holder, or importer of record. Regulatory clearances are held by device manufacturers. Buyers must verify compliance requirements in their jurisdiction. Not medical or regulatory advice.

3. Quote & Proforma Invoice

Use: All outgoing quotes, proforma invoices, pricing sheets

This quotation is issued by Asaptic (HK) Ltd acting as principal-reseller. Asaptic is not the device manufacturer, regulatory licence holder (MDALL/ARTG or equivalent), or importer of record in Canada, Australia, or any other jurisdiction. The quoted price does not include import duties, taxes, customs clearance fees, or in-country regulatory costs unless expressly stated as DDP. Regulatory clearance numbers referenced are held by the device manufacturer or their appointed regulatory agent. Buyer is responsible for confirming that the device is appropriately cleared/registered for use in the destination country, and for nominating a licensed customs broker and importer of record. This quote is valid for [14/30] days from the date of issue. Prices are in USD unless stated otherwise and subject to foreign exchange fluctuation.

Paste above the signature line on all Q-series documents. Replace [14/30] with applicable validity period.

Use: All commercial invoices accompanying shipments

All regulatory device clearances are held by the manufacturer or their appointed sponsor. The declared value represents the transaction value between Asaptic and the buyer only.
