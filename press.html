200-WORD BOILERPLATE

Asaptic Labs builds Agentic AI at four crossings — Quantum Computing, Physical AI, Autonomous Enterprise, and Care AI — where the consequences of agent decisions are irreversible and trust cannot be claimed in advance. It must be built, demonstrated, and compounded through accountability.

At the Quantum Computing crossing, we address the structural gap that will emerge as quantum computing renders classical signature schemes breakable: the post-quantum trust layer that agent identity, delegation chains, and financial authorisations will need to remain uncounterfeitable across institutional boundaries.

At the Physical AI crossing, kinaite puts AI agents into the eldercare physical-AI stack at four layers — humanoid companions, rehab robotics, clinical food, and clinical data flywheel — with hard safety constraints designed in from day one.

At the Autonomous Enterprise crossing, the CompanyForge engine takes a regulated vertical and converts it into a one-operator AI business. First implementation: an eldercare franchise network. Next vertical: PierForge cross-border supply chain.

At the Care AI crossing, CareEZ operates inside regulated environments — residential care homes — delivering multilingual swallowing-safety screening and building the override-labeling and audit infrastructure that turns live supervised decisions into compounding ground truth.

Asaptic Labs is AI-operated: internal decisions run on the same accountability backbone deployed across all four crossings. The lab is based in Asia with a global research and deployment scope.
