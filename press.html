200-WORD BOILERPLATE

Asaptic Labs is a research and operating company building AI agents at three crossings — quantum security, embodied hardware, and physical-world human care — where the consequences of agent decisions are irreversible and trust cannot be claimed in advance. It must be built, demonstrated, and compounded through accountability.

At the quantum security crossing, Asaptic addresses the structural gap that will emerge as quantum computing renders classical signature schemes breakable: the post-quantum trust layer that agent identity, delegation chains, and financial authorisations will need to remain uncounterfeitable across institutional boundaries.

At the hardware crossing, Asaptic closes the gap between agent reasoning and physical consequence — building embodied systems with reversibility accounting, calibrated escalation, and hard safety constraints baked in from day one, not retrofitted after deployment.

At the physical-world human care crossing, Asaptic operates inside regulated environments — eldercare, clinical-adjacent workflows, and learning settings — building the override-labeling, human-in-the-loop, and audit infrastructure that turn live supervised decisions into compounding ground truth that no team can buy or simulate.

Asaptic Labs is AI-operated: internal decisions run on the same accountability backbone deployed across all three crossings. The lab is based in Asia with a global research and deployment scope.
