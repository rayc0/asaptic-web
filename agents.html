FAQ

Trusted AI procurement, answered

What is Asaptic Agents?

Asaptic Agents is a trusted AI procurement agent for compliance-sensitive cross-border physical goods. It helps buyers source from Greater China to global markets while maintaining auditable evidence for supplier checks, UFLPA exposure, tariffs, certifications and quality.

How is Asaptic different from Alibaba Accio?

Asaptic is neutral and buyer-side. It does not leak buyer identity, demand signals or order volume to supplier marketplaces. It also works with your own suppliers, not only platform-listed ones, and builds an end-to-end evidence trail for compliance-sensitive procurement.

Is my sourcing data and buyer identity kept private?

Yes. Asaptic is built for confidential procurement where buyer identity, target pricing, supplier lists, order volume and compliance findings stay private. The agent acts for the buyer and preserves auditable records without broadcasting demand to the market.

Which goods and verticals does it support?

Compliance-sensitive cross-border physical goods — batteries and energy storage, electronics and hardware, industrial parts, medical and wellness products, food and packaging — where supplier, UFLPA, tariff, certification or quality checks matter.
