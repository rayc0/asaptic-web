Privacy

LAST UPDATED · 2026-05-18

What we collect

Nothing beyond standard Cloudflare edge logs — IP address, user agent, and country — retained briefly for security and abuse prevention. No analytics. No cookies, except a single localStorage entry named asaptic-lang that remembers your language preference.

Why we collect it

Security and language detection. That is the entire list.

Third parties

Cloudflare hosts the site and serves edge traffic. Google Fonts delivers Inter and JetBrains Mono. There are no tracking pixels, no ad networks, and no analytics providers.

Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@asaptic.com.

隐私

最后更新 · 2026-05-18

我们收集什么

仅限标准的 Cloudflare 边缘日志 —— IP 地址、浏览器标识、国家/地区 —— 用于安全防护与防滥用，短期保存。不使用任何分析工具。不设 Cookie，仅在浏览器的 localStorage 中保存一项 asaptic-lang，用于记忆您的语言偏好。

为什么收集

仅用于安全与语言识别。除此之外没有其他用途。

第三方

Cloudflare 提供网站托管与边缘流量服务。Google Fonts 提供 Inter 与 JetBrains Mono 字体。本网站不使用任何追踪像素、广告网络或分析服务。

如有疑问或请求：privacy@asaptic.com。
