Class I vs Class II

Consumer fitness scales and wellness products are often Class I or MDEL-only products and do not appear in MDALL. Clinical Class II body-composition analyzers require a Medical Device Licence, appear in MDALL when licensed, and should be checked by licence number, company name, device name, class, and active status before use in a procurement file.

How to verify a licence

Open Health Canada's MDALL search at mdall-limh.hc-sc.gc.ca. Search by company name or licence number, then check whether the record is active or inactive, whether the class is II, and whether the device name and licence holder match the supplier's documents.

For procurement due diligence, save the licence number, company name, device name, class, status, and date checked in the sourcing file. A current MDALL match should then be connected with clinical devices sourcing, the compliance matrix, supplier quality evidence, service coverage, and post-market support review.

Multi-market buyers can compare this Canada directory with the TGA-approved bioimpedance analyzers directory and the Health Canada MDALL vs TGA ARTG procurement guide. An MDALL record is a licensing signal, not a substitute for intended-use review, clinical validation review, ISO 13485 evidence, IEC 60601-1 documentation, or contract terms that cover recalls and field safety notices.

Frequently asked questions

Which body-composition and bioimpedance devices appear in Health Canada MDALL?

Public MDALL records include active Class II licences for body-composition or bioimpedance devices from InBody, Conmo Electronic, Omron, Blay Scientific, and Fresenius Medical Care, plus archived Seca and Tanita records that must not be treated as current active licences. Buyers should confirm each current record at mdall-limh.hc-sc.gc.ca before procurement.

Do consumer fitness scales appear in MDALL?

No. Consumer fitness scales that are Class I or MDEL-only products do not appear in MDALL. Clinical Class II body-composition analyzers require a Medical Device Licence and appear in MDALL when licensed.

How do buyers verify a Health Canada MDALL licence?

Search mdall-limh.hc-sc.gc.ca by company name or licence number, then confirm the device name, manufacturer, class, and active or inactive status before using the record in a procurement file.

Does an MDALL record prove a device is appropriate for every clinical workflow?

No. MDALL confirms a medical device licence record, not suitability for every intended use. Procurement teams should also review scope, labelling, clinical validation, ISO 13485 evidence, IEC 60601-1 evidence, service support, and post-market obligations.

如何核实许可证

打开 Health Canada 的 MDALL 搜索 mdall-limh.hc-sc.gc.ca。按公司名称或许可证号检索，然后检查记录是有效还是非有效、类别是否为 II、设备名称和许可证持有人是否与供应商文件一致。

采购尽调时，应在采购档案中保存许可证号、公司名称、设备名称、类别、状态和核查日期。当前 MDALL 匹配还应与临床设备采购、合规矩阵、供应商质量证据、服务覆盖和上市后支持审查结合。

常见问题

哪些体成分和生物电阻抗设备出现在 Health Canada MDALL 中？

公开 MDALL 记录包括 InBody、Conmo Electronic、Omron、Blay Scientific 和 Fresenius Medical Care 的体成分或生物电阻抗设备有效 II 类许可证，也包括 Seca 和 Tanita 已归档记录，后者不得作为当前有效许可证使用。

消费者健身秤会出现在 MDALL 中吗？

不会。I 类或仅 MDEL 的消费者健身秤不会出现在 MDALL 中。临床 II 类体成分分析仪需要 Medical Device Licence，并在获证后出现在 MDALL。

采购方如何核实 Health Canada MDALL 许可证？

按公司名称或许可证号搜索 mdall-limh.hc-sc.gc.ca，然后在采购档案引用前确认设备名称、制造商、类别以及有效或非有效状态。

MDALL 记录是否证明设备适合所有临床工作流？

不是。MDALL 确认医疗器械许可证记录，不证明适合每一种预期用途。采购团队还应审查范围、标签、临床验证、ISO 13485、IEC 60601-1、服务支持和上市后义务。
