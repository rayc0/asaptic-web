Short answer

Mexico is not a CCC carry-over market: three-phase induction motors must be screened against NOM-016-ENER-2016 under CONUEE, mapped to applicable electrical safety NOMs such as NOM-001-SEDE and NOM-003-SCFI, and certified through an approved body such as ANCE or NYCE where the NOM scope requires it.
