Short answer

The main Argentina compliance gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are: ENACOM mandatory homologación under Ley Argentina Digital 27.078 — all radio and telecom terminal equipment requires an ENACOM certificate and homologation number on the label before import or sale, with no recognition of FCC, CE, SRRC, or CCC approvals; an Argentine-registered importer with a valid CUIT number required for customs clearance; IRAM S-mark electrical safety certification and IRAM-IEC EMC compliance; product adaptation to the unique Argentine type-I plug per IRAM 2073 and 220V/50Hz power system; and Spanish language labelling. MERCOSUR mutual recognition with Brazil for wireless equipment is incomplete. Cybersecurity certification is currently advisory only.
