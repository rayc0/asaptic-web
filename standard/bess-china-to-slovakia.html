Short answer

For Slovakia, the practical entry barriers are mainly EU CE conformity execution (LVD 2014/35/EU, EMC 2014/30/EU, RED 2014/53/EU where applicable, plus Battery Regulation (EU) 2023/1542 lifecycle and due-diligence duties), ÚRSO/SEPS grid-interconnection documentation, and ÚNMS/market-acceptance alignment. EU membership makes this a harmonised-framework-first market, with 230/400 V 50 Hz utility conditions. Slovakia is landlocked; primary import logistics run via Hamburg or Koper ports with overland transit. Slovak-language documentation is often expected for project permits and installation approvals.
