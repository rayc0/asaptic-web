Short answer

Netherlands is a full EU member state: RED 2014/53/EU applies without national derogation, enforced by RDI (Rijksinspectie Digitale Infrastructuur). CE marking covering radio performance (EN 300 328 / EN 301 893), EMC (EN 301 489), electrical safety (EN IEC 62368-1:2020+A11), and cybersecurity under RED Art. 3.3(d)-(f) (EN 18031, mandatory from 1 August 2025) are required. An EU Authorised Representative is mandatory for Chinese manufacturers. Rotterdam (Europe's largest port) is a documented high-risk entry point: RDI conducts active customs inspection programs targeting Chinese-origin wireless electronics at Rotterdam — non-compliant goods are detained or destroyed at port.
