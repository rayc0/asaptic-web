Short answer

Filtering facepiece respirators entering Botswana are regulated primarily through the Botswana Bureau of Standards (BOBS), which operates conformity assessment and import-inspection under the BOS mark/standards-mark scheme and adopts international/regional standards (commonly EN 149 FFP1/FFP2/FFP3 and SANS/IEC-aligned standards). Workplace respiratory protection also sits within Botswana's occupational-safety framework. Chinese KN95 (GB 2626) and GB 19083 test reports are not automatically recognised; the importer of record typically arranges BOBS conformity/inspection against the adopted EN 149 requirements. Botswana is landlocked, so goods route via Durban (South Africa) or Walvis Bay (Namibia).
