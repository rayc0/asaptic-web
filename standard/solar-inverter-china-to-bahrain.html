Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Bahrain, public sources do not show one universal mandatory whole-product certificate at the product-import level, but EWA material-approval listing is required for inverters used in net-metering or grid-connected installations. IEC 62109-1 / IEC 62109-2 safety evidence is the international baseline expected by EWA-approved consultants and project stakeholders. The main gaps versus China documentation are the EWA material-approval route, 50 Hz grid settings (Bahrain operates at 50 Hz / 230 V), and anti-islanding and voltage/frequency ride-through settings to EWA grid-code tolerances.
