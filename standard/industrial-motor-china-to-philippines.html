Short answer

For covered three-phase industrial AC motors, the Philippines gap is usually not only IE class: DOE IE2 minimum, BPS/DTI PS Mark or ICC import clearance, PNS/IEC 60034 evidence, and true 60 Hz design must be confirmed. China 50 Hz motors should be treated as not directly compatible without 60 Hz rating and validation.
