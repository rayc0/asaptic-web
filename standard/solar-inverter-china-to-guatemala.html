Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Guatemala, the key practical gaps are: (1) confirming COGUANOR-adopted standards and conformity documentation expected for the product; (2) satisfying CNEE and distributor interconnection requirements for Generación Distribuida Renovable or, for larger market projects, applicable AMM connection and registration requirements; (3) providing IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence; (4) providing IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 grid-interface evidence configured for 60 Hz operation; (5) providing IEC 61000 / IEC 62920 EMC evidence; and (6) reconfiguring and retesting protection settings for Guatemala's 120/240 V, 60 Hz grid. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 evidence is not automatically accepted and is typically 50 Hz / 220-380 V based.
