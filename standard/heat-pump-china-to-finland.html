Short answer

CE marking under LVD and EMC Directive, Ecodesign seasonal efficiency (ηs ≥ 125% with EN 14825 cold-climate test points at -15°C/-22°C), F-gas GWP caps and Tukes-recognized technician qualification, Pressure Equipment Directive classification, and EPREL energy label registration are the major Finland compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps.
