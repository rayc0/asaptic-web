Short answer

For China-made BESS exported to Albania, the practical baseline is CE-aligned product compliance where the equipment is in scope of low-voltage, EMC, radio, and safety-related regimes, with TS EN IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence commonly used for battery and system safety. UN 38.3 is required for lithium-battery transport. Grid-connection and operational review is separate and handled by ERE and OST for project category and connection point.
