Short answer

Kazakhstan is in the EAEU, so China-exported lithium batteries and power banks commonly need EAC-based conformity, TR CU safety and EMC compliance, TR EAEU 037/2016 hazardous-substance evidence, and local in-country importer handling; Chinese GB 31241 and CCC do not automatically satisfy Kazakhstan market-entry routes.
