Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Ethiopia, the baseline is conformity with Ethiopian standards (ES), which commonly adopt IEC 62841, verified through ESA national conformity and CACEA / Ministry of Trade import inspection at entry via Djibouti; cordless tools with radio functions also need ECA radio approval. There is no EU-style mandatory outdoor-noise product marking in Ethiopia, so noise should be treated as a design and labour-safety consideration, not a product CE-equivalent marking obligation.
