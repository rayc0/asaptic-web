Short answer

KC electrical safety is compulsory where the EV charger is in scope; Korean deployments also require Korea-market connector compatibility (Type 1/CCS1, not GB/T 20234), KEPCO grid acceptance, and Ministry of Environment/KECO public-charging rules.
