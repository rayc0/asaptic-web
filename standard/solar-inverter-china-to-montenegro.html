Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Montenegro, the practical gates are CE-oriented conformity for in-scope low-voltage and EMC requirements, EN/IEC safety evidence such as IEC 62109, REGAGEN and CGES project and interconnection review, anti-islanding and protection settings, and evidence that China GB/T 37408 and GB/T 19964 are technical baseline references only, not market authorization in Montenegro.
