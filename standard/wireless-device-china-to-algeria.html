Short answer

ARPCE mandatory homologation under Loi n° 18-04 relative aux communications électroniques is the primary gap for any wireless or telecom terminal device exported from China to Algeria. CE mark, CCC, and SRRC approval are not recognised by ARPCE; a separate Algerian homologation process with ETSI/IEC-referenced test reports is required. Additional gaps include IANOR/NA-aligned EMC standards, 230 V / 50 Hz electrical safety with Type C/F plug, a locally established Algerian authorised importer, bilingual Arabic and French product labelling, import licensing and currency-control compliance with Direction des Douanes, and ANSSSI cybersecurity advisory guidelines.
