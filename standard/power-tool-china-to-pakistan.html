Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Pakistan, the baseline is product safety to PS/IEC 62841 (Pakistan adopts IEC), PSQCA conformity and marking where the item is on the PSQCA Conformity Assessment list, PTA type approval for the radio module in cordless or wireless tools, and an in-country importer; Pakistan has no EU-style horizontal RoHS, battery, or outdoor-noise marking regime, so those EU duties do not transpose directly.
