Short answer

Brazil market entry for Chinese heat pumps is blocked mainly by mandatory INMETRO certification, mandatory PROCEL / ENCE energy labelling before sale, IBAMA refrigerant import licensing and HFC quota controls, ANATEL obligations for wireless modules, and a critical 60 Hz plus 127 V / 220 V redesign gap. China CCC, GB 25454, GB/T 7725, and GB 9237 documentation are useful baselines but are not recognized as Brazil approvals.
