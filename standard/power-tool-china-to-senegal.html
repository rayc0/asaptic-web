Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Senegal, the practical baseline is conformity to ASN-adopted NS/IEC 62841 electrical-safety standards plus the Ministry of Trade PEC (Programme d'Evaluation de la Conformite) / Verification of Conformity regime for import inspection, with French-language documentation; cordless tools with embedded radio additionally need ARTP authorisation. Senegal generally adopts IEC standards and does not run an EU-style horizontal RoHS, battery, or outdoor-noise product regime, so those EU obligations do not transpose directly.
