Short answer

For lithium cells, battery packs, and power banks exported from China to Indonesia, the key requirement is mandatory in-country compliance. Products in mandatory-SNI scope need BSN-recognized conformity assessment and SPPT-SNI marking, wireless models need SDPPI radio/telecom approval, and imported products generally require API-U / NIB-based in-country import registration support. UN 38.3 remains required for transport and is usually present in China export practice but must be paired with Indonesia-side logistics and customs documentation.
