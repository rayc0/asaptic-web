Short answer

For Mexico, the main China-to-market gaps are mandatory NOM electrical safety review under NOM-001-SEDE installation rules and NOM-003-SCFI product safety, certification through an approved body such as NYCE or ANCE where applicable, redesign away from GB/T 20234 toward SAE J1772 and CCS1, CRE/CFE grid connection compatibility, and Spanish labelling.
