Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers face four major Turkey compliance gaps: (1) electrical safety re-testing to TS EN 60335-2-24 for CE marking — adopted by Turkey through the EU-Turkey Customs Union — as CCC/GB 4706.13 certification is not accepted; (2) Turkish energy-efficiency label (EU-aligned A-G scale) and energy-efficiency declaration must be prepared to the Turkish regime administered by the Ministry of Trade; (3) EMC re-testing to TS EN 55014-1 — Chinese GB 4343.1 CCC test data cannot substitute; and (4) appointment of a Turkey-established importer responsible for product compliance and Ministry of Trade market surveillance. R-600a refrigerant is well-positioned for compliance but charge documentation against TS EN 60335-2-24 Annex AA must be verified. Goods typically clear through Mersin or Istanbul ports. Grid is 230/400 V, 50 Hz (50 Hz matches China; voltage differs from China's 220/380 V).
