Short answer

The US has no single federal power-tool mark. For practical market access, Chinese power-tool exporters usually need NRTL listing to UL 62841 or legacy UL 60745 for electric shock and fire safety, FCC Part 15 authorization if electronics or wireless functions are present, separate battery-pack evidence such as UL 2595 or UL 2271 where applicable, plus OSHA workplace and CPSC general product-safety readiness.
