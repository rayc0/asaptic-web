Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers face several Azerbaijan compliance gaps: (1) mandatory conformity confirmation under the AZSTAND (State Service for Antimonopoly and Consumer Market Control) regime — CCC/GB 4706.13 certification is not directly accepted, and electrical safety must be demonstrated against AZS/IEC 60335-2-24; (2) Azerbaijan is NOT an EAEU member and operates a national conformity regime, so EAC marking does not substitute for AZSTAND conformity; (3) EMC demonstration against IEC/CISPR-based AZS standards; (4) energy efficiency declaration under national energy programmes; (5) appointment of an in-country importer or responsible representative for customs clearance and conformity. The genuine advantage: Azerbaijan's 220/380 V 50 Hz grid matches China's nominal 220/380 V 50 Hz, so no re-engineering for voltage or frequency is required. R-600a refrigerant is well-positioned but charge documentation against AZS/IEC 60335-2-24 must be verified.
