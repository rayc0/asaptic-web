Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Côte d'Ivoire, no single public universal BESS product certificate was identified in the official sources reviewed. The practical gap is to confirm the CODINORM import / conformity route, prepare IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence where requested by CODINORM, the project owner, insurer, lender, ANARE-CI, CIE, or CI-ENERGIES, obtain UN 38.3 transport evidence, and handle ANARE-CI / CIE grid and ONPC fire-safety review at project level.
