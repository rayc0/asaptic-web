Short answer

For solar inverters exported from China to Moldova, key practical gaps are CE/EN-oriented product documentation, ANRE project interconnection conditions, anti-islanding settings for local operation, electrical installation conditions for a 230/400 V 50 Hz power system, and transport documentation for a landlocked route through Romania and Giurgiulești corridor. This does not replace in-use project tests at the target site.
