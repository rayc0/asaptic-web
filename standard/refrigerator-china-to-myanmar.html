Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers entering Myanmar should prepare MSTRD conformity documentation mapped to MS/IEC 60335-2-24, importer-held technical files, and MIC or Ministry of Commerce import documentation for the applicable HS code. Myanmar uses 230 V / 50 Hz, so the 50 Hz frequency matches China but the nominal voltage differs from China's 220/380 V baseline and should be verified on ratings and test reports. MEPE-linked energy programmes and buyer requirements may ask for energy-consumption evidence even where a mandatory refrigerator label is not confirmed. R-600a is a practical refrigerant choice if charge and flammability documentation are clear. Myanmar does not have a horizontal RoHS regime, so do not create a Myanmar RoHS declaration; keep China RoHS or material data only for buyer due diligence.
