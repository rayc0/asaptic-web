Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Chile, the main gap is not a simple China GB/T-to-Chile equivalence. SEC authorization for photovoltaic products and installation declaration routes, IEC 62109 safety evidence, Chilean 50 Hz grid settings, CNE / Coordinador connection rules, and project-specific PMGD or distributed-generation procedures must be addressed before the inverter is used in a Chilean grid-connected PV project.
