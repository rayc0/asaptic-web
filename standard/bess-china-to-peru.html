Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Peru, the main requirements are: (1) IEC 62619 safety conformity for secondary lithium cells and batteries used in industrial BESS applications (NTP-IEC 62619 adoption by INACAL is the reference framework); (2) IEC 62933 series for grid energy storage system performance and safety (NTP-IEC 62933 where adopted); (3) UN 38.3 transport testing for lithium cells and batteries; (4) 60 Hz validation for all PCS, transformers, and balance-of-system equipment; (5) Osinergmin technical approval and COES/SEIN grid-connection requirements for utility-scale storage projects. China GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023 documents support technical due diligence but do not replace Peru-specific conformity routes.
