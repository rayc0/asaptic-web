Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Colombia, do not treat a China GB/T 18487 or GB/T 20234 file as direct Colombia compliance. The practical file should first check RETIE electrical-safety and installation scope, then map IEC 61851 / IEC 62196 expectations where specified by the project, address Colombia's 60 Hz grid and distribution-utility connection requirements, and keep Ley 1964 incentives separate from product approval.
