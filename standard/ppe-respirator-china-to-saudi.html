Short answer

For Saudi Arabia, the highest-impact commercial gaps are SABER PCoC and per-consignment SCoC documents, Saudi-recognized EN 149/NIOSH-linked performance claims for filtering respirators, mandatory in-country importer flow, and Arabic safety labelling. GB 2626-2019 or GB 19083 certificates are technical baselines in China, not direct substitutions for Saudi compliance workflow.
