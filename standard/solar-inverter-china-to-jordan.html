Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Jordan, do not rely only on China GB/T or generic IEC files. The practical package should confirm JSMO standard applicability, IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence, EMRC renewable-energy connection instructions, NEPCO or distribution-company grid acceptance, the project route for net-metering or wheeling where applicable, 50 Hz settings, and a China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 crosswalk.
