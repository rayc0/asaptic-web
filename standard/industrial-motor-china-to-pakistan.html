Short answer

For Pakistan, the practical gaps are PSQCA conformity for listed motor products, IEC 60034-based documentation, NEECA energy-efficiency direction using IE classes, and 400/230 V 50 Hz grid suitability; China CCC or GB 18613 evidence is not a Pakistan approval by itself.
