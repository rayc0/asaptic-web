Short answer

For lithium batteries and power banks from China to the Philippines, the practical gate is Philippine in-country compliance. BPS PS or ICC evidence and any required import-side marking are destination-specific. Wireless-enabled products often trigger NTC filing. UN 38.3 remains the transport core, while Philippine electrical context is usually 230 V 60 Hz, so design and labels should be checked accordingly.
