Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Senegal, the practical gap is usually not a confirmed blanket whole-unit Senegal certificate. The main work is to map China GB/T 18487 / GB/T 20234 evidence to any ASN-adopted or tender-specified IEC 61851 / IEC 62196 route, redesign GB/T connectors where an IEC connector interface is required, confirm 230 V / 400 V 50 Hz site supply and protection settings, and obtain CRSE / Senelec / project-owner acceptance for grid connection and public operation where applicable.
