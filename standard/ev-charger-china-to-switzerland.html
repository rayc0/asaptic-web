Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Switzerland, the practical gap is separating product conformity from site and service readiness. Focus on CE technical-file route under SNV-aligned standards, IEC 61851-1 / 62124 for electrical safety, Type 2 and CCS2 connector configuration, OCPP interoperability for backend operations, Swissgrid and operator-side grid coordination, and 230/400 V 50 Hz project setup.
