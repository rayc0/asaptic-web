Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Pakistan, do not assume a China GB/T report alone is enough. The practical file should confirm PSQCA scope and any Pakistan Standard or IEC 62109 adoption route, prepare IEC 62109 safety evidence, support the customer's current NEPRA / DISCO grid-connection or prosumer / net-metering route, validate 50 Hz and Pakistan protection settings, include EMC evidence, and map China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 evidence only as a technical baseline.
