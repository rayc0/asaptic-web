Short answer

Sweden fully applies the EU Radio Equipment Directive (RED 2014/53/EU); CE marking under RED is the primary gate enforced by PTS (Post- och telestyrelsen). Key gaps versus Chinese SRRC/CCC include: radio performance testing to EN 300 328 / EN 301 893 (with mandatory 5 GHz DFS — PTS is a well-documented enforcer of DFS violations), EMC to EN 301 489 series, electrical safety to EN IEC 62368-1:2020+A11 (230 V/50 Hz, plug type C/F Schuko), cybersecurity under RED Art. 3.3(d)–(f) via EN 18031 (mandatory from 1 August 2025; Sweden is a high-uptake EU Cyber Resilience Act market), EU Authorised Representative, Swedish-language labelling for consumer products, and WEEE registration in Sweden via El-kretsen. CCC and FCC are not recognised.
