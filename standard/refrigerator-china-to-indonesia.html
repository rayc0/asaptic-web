Short answer

For refrigerators from China to Indonesia, assume a dual control path: mandatory Indonesian SNI route with importer-linked market-access documents, and separate energy and safety evidence in the Indonesia format. LSPro-processed SNI marking, ESDM label compliance, and the in-country importer requirement are typically the highest-friction points because China CCC documents and domestic labels do not complete Indonesian import readiness by themselves.
