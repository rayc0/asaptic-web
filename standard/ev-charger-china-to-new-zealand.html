Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering New Zealand, the practical gaps are WorkSafe / Energy Safety electrical product evidence, RCM / EESS-style compliance records, installation under the Electricity (Safety) Regulations 2010 by a licensed New Zealand electrician, compatibility with New Zealand's 230/400 V 50 Hz supply and AS/NZS 3000 wiring rules, and hardware redesign from China GB/T 20234 connectors to IEC 62196 Type 2 for AC and CCS2 for DC. GB/T connector chargers are physically incompatible with the New Zealand direction and cannot be fixed by paperwork alone.
