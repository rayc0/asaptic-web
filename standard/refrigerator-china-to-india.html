Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers face five major India compliance gaps: (1) BIS market access under the BIS Act framework, including product-family review against refrigerator standards such as IS 1476 and IS 7872 where applicable; (2) BEE star labelling permission and model registration for frost-free and direct-cool refrigerators before sale; (3) electrical safety evidence against the IS 302 series, with Indian Standards used as conformity benchmarks rather than automatically interchangeable with Chinese CCC test data; (4) refrigerant and foam-blowing-agent review under India's Ozone Depleting Substances rules, especially for HCFC/HFC legacy models; and (5) import clearance alignment with BIS/BEE registration documents, Indian importer responsibility, labels, model identifiers, and test reports.

Electrical Safety — IS 302 Series for Household Appliances China's refrigerator safety evidence is commonly built around CCC and GB 4706.13 with GB 4706.1. The Chinese test basis is related to the IEC 60335 family but includes China-specific deviations, marking, plug, supply, and certification controls. India requires India-specific evidence and cannot rely on the existence of a CCC certificate alone.China CCC framework — domestic appliance safety certification route
GB 4706.1 and GB 4706.13 — Chinese appliance safety test bases		 India uses the IS 302 household-appliance safety series as a core technical basis for electrical safety of household and similar appliances, including refrigerator-relevant general safety requirements and particular requirements where applicable. Where Indian law, BIS orders, QCO scope, licence/registration conditions, or importer obligations specify an Indian Standard, that standard is compulsory for in-scope products. Key areas include protection against electric shock, insulation, leakage current, earthing, temperature rise, abnormal operation, mechanical strength, supply cord, marking, and instructions.Bureau of Indian Standards Act, 2016 — legal basis for BIS conformity assessment
BIS Conformity Assessment Regulations, 2018 — licence, registration, and surveillance framework
IS 302 series — household and similar electrical appliance safety standard compulsory where specified for products within QCO/BIS scope		 The gap is not only test clauses; it includes India plug and supply assumptions, marking language, importer identity, rated voltage/frequency, instruction manual content, critical component approvals, factory quality controls, and surveillance response.
