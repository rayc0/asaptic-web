Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters to Mexico should treat NOM compliance as a separate market-access project: in-scope power banks and electronic products may require NOM-001-SCFI safety certification through NYCE or another approved body, Spanish commercial labelling under NOM-050-SCFI, and UN 38.3/SCT dangerous-goods transport evidence. Chinese GB 31241, CCC, UN 38.3, or CNAS reports may support the file but do not replace Mexican NOM certification or Spanish labelling.
