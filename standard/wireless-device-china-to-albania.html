Short answer

AKEP mandatory type approval certificate (not substitutable by CE DoC, CCC, or FCC alone), EN/ETSI-based EMC testing accepted by AKEP via DSMK-adopted standards, EN IEC 62368-1 electrical safety for 230 V / 50 Hz / Type C/F, appointment of a local Albanian importer/authorised representative, Albanian language labelling, and emerging cybersecurity alignment with EU RED are the major compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC radio type approval, CCC certification, and GB standards. CCC, FCC, and CE DoC alone are not recognised by AKEP as substitutes for Albanian type approval.
