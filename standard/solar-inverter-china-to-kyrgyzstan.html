Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Kyrgyzstan, the baseline route is not China GB/T acceptance. Kyrgyzstan is an EAEU member, so in-scope electrical/electronic inverters commonly need EAEU conformity under TR CU 004/2011 low-voltage equipment and TR CU 020/2011 EMC with EAC marking before placement on the market. Project delivery then depends on Kyrgyzstandart / GOST / IEC evidence, grid-operator connection approval through NEHC subsidiaries such as NESK distribution companies or NEGK transmission, IEC 62109 safety, IEC 62116 anti-islanding, IEC 61727 utility-interface behavior, and firmware validation for 220/380 V at 50 Hz, with additional attention to mountainous high-altitude and cold-climate derating.
