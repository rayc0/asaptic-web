Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Tanzania, the mandatory baseline is conformity with TBS national standards (which adopt IEC/EN, including the IEC 62841 series) verified through the TBS Pre-Export Verification of Conformity (PVoC) scheme, a Certificate of Conformity at the port of entry such as Dar es Salaam, and use of the TBS mark; cordless tools with radio functions also require TCRA type approval. China GB/T 3883 evidence is the domestic baseline but does not by itself satisfy these obligations.
