Short answer

Morocco adopts NM IEC 60034 standards via IMANOR and aligns efficiency direction to IEC 60034-30-1 under ANRE/PNEE programs; CE marking and EU DoC are broadly accepted due to Morocco-EU Advanced Status. Key gaps are the 400 V (vs China 380 V) grid voltage and confirming the applicable conformity route (IMANOR NM mark vs CE DoC acceptance).
