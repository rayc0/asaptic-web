Short answer

For China-to-Moldova BESS exports, the practical baseline is a CE/EN-oriented conformity pathway where in-scope electrical, EMC, and energy-safety obligations are built around applicable Moldovan-adopted international standards and ANRE project requirements. Moldova shares 50 Hz with China but nominal voltage is 230/400 V rather than China’s 220/380 V, so inverter and protection settings should be project-verified. Additional work is usually on ANRE/Moldelectrica grid-connection documentation, ISM-based product safety documentation, fire-safety/installation acceptance, and transport documentation for the landlocked route to Giurgiulești with Romanian transfer corridors.
