Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering the Philippines, the main practical gaps are DOE EVIDA charging-station obligations, BPS/PNS or IEC 61851 evidence where specified by procurement or authorities, IEC 62196 connector interoperability rather than China GB/T 20234 hardware, 60 Hz grid operation, EMC testing for off-board EV supply equipment, and alignment from the China GB/T 18487 baseline. A blanket compulsory whole-unit BPS certification requirement for every EV charger model should not be assumed without confirming the current BPS technical-regulation scope.
