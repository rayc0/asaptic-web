Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Indonesia, the biggest practical gaps are not a confirmed blanket whole-unit SNI certificate, but SNI/IEC technical alignment, ESDM SPKLU business and operation rules, PLN utility coordination, GB/T-to-IEC connector redesign, TKDN local-content expectations for public procurement or incentive-linked projects, and safety / EMC evidence. Whole-unit mandatory certification should be verified with BSN and the responsible Indonesian authorities for the exact model and use case.

Factory conformity reports		 Indonesia's TKDN system records domestic-content values and is administered through Indonesian industry-ministry systems. Project-specific procurement or incentive documents		 TKDN is a supply-chain and procurement gap.
