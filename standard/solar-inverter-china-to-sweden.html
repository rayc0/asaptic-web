Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Sweden, practical gaps are: confirm the applicable SIS SS-EN route and project-level approval path with Ei and Svenska kraftnät; validate that IEC 62109-1/-2 safety testing, IEC 62116 anti-islanding, IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence, and IEC 62920 / SS-EN 61000 EMC documentation are accepted for Swedish installations; and prove 230/400 V 50 Hz performance, SS-EN 50549 grid-code protection thresholds, reconnection timing, and cold-climate derating for Sweden-connected projects. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 documents are useful but not automatically interchangeable with Swedish evidence.
