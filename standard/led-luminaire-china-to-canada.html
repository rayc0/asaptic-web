Short answer

A Chinese LED luminaire manufacturer entering Canada should plan for Canadian electrical safety approval through an SCC-accredited certification body using the CSA C22.2 No. 250 series or the applicable Canadian national standard; ISED compliance for interference-causing equipment under ICES requirements; NRCan energy efficiency reporting and marking where the product is a covered lamp or contains a regulated lamp; and English/French consumer-facing labelling, with French prominence checked for Quebec. China CCC, GB 7000.1, China RoHS, and Chinese-language reports do not replace Canadian certification, Canadian importer responsibility, or bilingual labelling.
