Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Morocco, the critical gap is the connector standard: Morocco's IMANOR pathway aligns with IEC 62196-2 (Type 2, Mennekes) for AC charging and IEC 62196-3 (CCS Combo 2) for DC fast charging, whereas Chinese products commonly use GB/T 20234.2 (AC) and GB/T 20234.3 (DC) connectors, which are not interchangeable. Charger safety follows IEC 61851 as adopted by IMANOR NM, and ONEE grid-connection rules under loi 13-09 apply for public or semi-public infrastructure. A hardware connector change plus IEC 61851 test report is typically required.
