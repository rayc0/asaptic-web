Short answer

China-market refrigerator files do not carry over automatically to Mexico. The main gaps are NOM-015-ENER energy-efficiency testing and label alignment, NOM-003-SCFI or NOM-001-SCFI electrical-safety scope review, a Mexico NOM certificate through an approved body such as NYCE or ANCE where applicable, refrigerant charge and marking evidence, and Spanish commercial information under NOM-024-SCFI. CCC, China Energy Label, GB 4706.13, and GB 12021.2 evidence can support engineering review but do not replace Mexican NOM obligations.
