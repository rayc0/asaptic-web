Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Mozambique, the key practical gaps are: (1) INNOQ conformity and import certification against Mozambique NM standards and adopted IEC evidence; (2) ARENE licensing for grid-connected, mini-grid, IPP, or regulated electricity activities; (3) EDM connection approval and protection-setting acceptance for the 220/380 V, 50 Hz network; (4) IEC 62109-1/-2 safety, IEC 62116 anti-islanding, IEC 61727 utility-interface, and IEC 61000 / IEC 62920 EMC test evidence; and (5) lender or donor specifications for financed solar, rural electrification, mining, and gas-sector projects. China GB/T 37408-2021, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 evidence should not be treated as automatically accepted in Mozambique without INNOQ, ARENE, EDM, and project-owner confirmation.
