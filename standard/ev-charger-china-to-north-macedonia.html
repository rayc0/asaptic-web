Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering North Macedonia, the main practical gaps are CE technical-file and declaration work for applicable EU-aligned product legislation, TS EN IEC 61851 technical alignment, GB/T-to-IEC Type 2 or CCS2 connector redesign, ERC charging-network licensing for public charging service operations, MEPSO and distribution-company grid project review, OCPP backend interoperability, and EMC / residual-current protection evidence. Do not assume a blanket whole-unit approval without checking the exact model, voltage, corridor route to Thessaloniki via Greece, installation route, and use case.
