Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering India, the mandatory baseline is BIS certification or CRS registration where the tool, charger, battery, or cell is covered by a notified QCO or CRS category; in-scope IS 302 or other relevant IS requirements are compulsory under the notification, not voluntary, while China GB 3883 evidence does not replace BIS registration, licensing, Standard Mark use, or import clearance duties.
