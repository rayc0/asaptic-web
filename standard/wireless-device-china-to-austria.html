Short answer

Radio performance (EN 300 328 / EN 301 893), EMC (EN 301 489), electrical safety (EN IEC 62368-1:2020+A11), and cybersecurity under RED Art. 3.3(d)-(f) mandatory from 1 August 2025 (EN 18031) are the major gaps compared with Chinese SRRC approval and CCC certification. Austria-specific requirements include: RTR (Rundfunk und Telekom Regulierungs-GmbH) as the national radio authority enforcing RED; German-language (Deutsch) product documentation, labelling, and Declaration of Conformity; Type F (Schuko) grounded plug compliance at 230 V/50 Hz; WEEE registration with EAK Austria (separate registry from Germany's EAR); and an EU Authorised Representative mandatory for non-EU manufacturers. CCC and FCC approvals are not recognised in Austria.
