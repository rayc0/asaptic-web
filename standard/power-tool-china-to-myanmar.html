Short answer

For China-made power tools entering Myanmar, the practical baseline is MSTRD conformity documentation where requested, IEC 60745 or IEC 62841 based safety evidence, Myanmar 230 V 50 Hz suitability, MIC or customs readiness for import and investment channels, and MPT review for wireless-enabled models; Myanmar does not have a horizontal RoHS regime like the EU.
