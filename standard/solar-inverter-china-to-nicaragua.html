Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Nicaragua, the key practical gaps are: (1) grid reconfiguration and retesting for Nicaragua's 60 Hz / 120-240 V distribution environment, not China's 50 Hz / 220-380 V baseline; (2) MEM/INE and ENATREL or distributor interconnection review for grid-tied projects; (3) Nicaraguan NTON/DGCN conformity context where a technical standard or project specification applies; (4) IEC 62109-1/-2 safety type-test evidence; (5) IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 grid-interface evidence validated at 60 Hz; and (6) IEC 62920 / IEC 61000 EMC evidence. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 evidence is useful background but does not automatically satisfy Nicaragua project acceptance.
