Short answer

For Australian workplace respiratory protection, respirators are expected to be certified to AS/NZS 1716 and used under an appropriate respiratory protection program, with selection, fit, training, maintenance, and risk controls driven by WHS duties. Medical or surgical masks are a different route: if supplied with therapeutic or surgical/infection-control claims, they are medical devices regulated by the TGA and generally require inclusion in the ARTG unless an exemption applies. Chinese KN95, GB 2626, GB 19083, or YY 0469 documentation does not automatically satisfy Australian AS/NZS 1716, WHS, TGA ARTG, or labelling requirements.
