Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Ghana, GSA conformity assessment and a Certificate of Conformity are required for imported electrical equipment at customs. IEC 62619 (lithium battery safety for industrial applications) and IEC 62933 (BESS system performance) are the relevant international benchmarks referenced by GSA. Energy Commission approval is required for grid-connected storage. UN 38.3 transport testing is mandatory for lithium cells and batteries in international transport. Chinese GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023 cover overlapping technical ground but are not accepted as substitutes for Ghana conformity documents or international transport requirements.
