Short answer

For China-made cordless or corded power tools, Argentina entry normally centers on a destination product-owner model through a CUIT-holding importer, with S-mark/IRAM electrical-safety expectations, ENACOM review for intentional radio, and market-specific evidence for battery and noise contexts; it does not have a single EU-style mandatory RoHS, battery-regime, or outdoor-noise-marking framework.
