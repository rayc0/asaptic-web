Short answer

MTPC radio type-approval is the primary mandatory gate for wireless devices entering Laos; DOSM IEC-based MS standards govern electrical safety and EMC on a voluntary-to-mandatory spectrum; MEM and EDL apply additional requirements for smart-energy radio interfaces; Laos has no formal cybersecurity framework for wireless devices as of mid-2026; and Laos has no horizontal RoHS equivalent. China MIIT type-approval, SRRC, and CCC certifications provide a useful technical baseline but do not substitute for Lao approvals.
