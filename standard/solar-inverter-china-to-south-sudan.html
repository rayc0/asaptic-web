Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to South Sudan, the key practical gaps are: (1) confirming whether SSNBS or the project owner requires IEC-based evidence because local product-specific rules are still developing; (2) IEC 62109-1/-2 safety type-test evidence; (3) IEC 62116 and IEC 61727 grid-interface evidence where a system connects to SSEC or a mini-grid; (4) IEC 62920 / IEC 61000 EMC evidence; and (5) validation for South Sudan's 50 Hz / 230/400 V nominal supply and severe heat conditions. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 evidence should not be treated as automatically accepted in South Sudan.
