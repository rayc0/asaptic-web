Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Chile, the mandatory baseline is SEC electrical product certification through an authorized certifier against the applicable NCh IEC standards (commonly the NCh IEC 62841 series); cordless tools with radio modules also need SUBTEL type approval. Chile adopts IEC-based standards via INN but has no EU-style horizontal RoHS, battery, or outdoor-noise regime.
