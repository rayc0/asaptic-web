Short answer

For Indonesia, SNI certification under BSN and KEMENDAG requirements plus ESDM mandatory energy labeling/MEPS are the practical entry gates, while KLHK refrigerant controls create additional pre-supply and product documentation obligations; 50 Hz plus 220 V single-phase and 380 V three-phase remains aligned with China.
