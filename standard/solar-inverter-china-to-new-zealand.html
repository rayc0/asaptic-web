Short answer

New Zealand does not use the Australian Clean Energy Council approved-inverter list. Market access depends on New Zealand electrical safety evidence, lawful RCM/EESS use where applicable, AS/NZS 4777.2:2020 New Zealand inverter settings, and approval by the local lines company under the Electricity Authority Part 6 process; Chinese GB/T or NB/T evidence is not a substitute.
