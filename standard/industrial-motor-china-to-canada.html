Short answer

The export-critical gap is not only certification: Canada uses 60 Hz and common 600/347 V three-phase industrial systems, so a China 50 Hz / 380 V motor can be electrically incompatible even before CSA certification and NRCan IE3 compliance are assessed. CCC is not recognized for Canadian market access.
