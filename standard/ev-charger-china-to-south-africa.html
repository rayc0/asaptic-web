Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering South Africa, the confirmed gaps are South Africa-specific NRCS LOA scope review where the charger or its components fall under compulsory electrotechnical specifications, SANS/IEC 61851 safety evidence, Type 2 or CCS2 connector strategy rather than GB/T 20234, local NRS or municipal grid-connection requirements, and EMC evidence suitable for the intended installation. Do not treat China GB/T 18487 or GB/T 20234 reports as automatic South Africa market access.
