Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering the UAE, the confirmed practical gaps are IEC-based safety and EMC evidence, connector redesign from GB/T 20234 to IEC 62196 Type 2 / CCS2 where required by the intended network, DEWA or local utility grid-connection acceptance, and OCPP back-office interoperability for networked chargers. A blanket UAE whole-unit mandatory EVSE certification rule could not be confirmed from official sources as of 2026-06-13, so exporters should treat MoIAT / ESMA conformity assessment as scope-dependent and verify the exact HS code, electrical-product category, and emirate-level project approval route.
