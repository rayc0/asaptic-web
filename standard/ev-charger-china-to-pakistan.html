Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Pakistan, the main gap is not a simple GB/T-to-PS mark substitution. Pakistan public EV charging stations must follow NEPRA's EV charging-station framework and applicable utility connection, metering, tariff, safety, signage, fire, IP, earthing, and connector expectations; the framework expressly points to PSQCA/NEECA-approved standards and international standards such as IEC, including IEC 61851-1 insulation and IEC 62196 Type 2 / CCS2 connector expectations. China GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 files are useful baseline evidence but do not replace Pakistan site approval or IEC connector conformity.
