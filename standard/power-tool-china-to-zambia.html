Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Zambia, the mandatory baseline is ZABS conformity assessment and import inspection with the ZABS mark, against ZS standards that adopt IEC (the ZS/IEC 62841 family for power-tool safety); cordless tools with radio functions also fall under ZICTA type approval. China GB/T 3883 evidence is a useful starting point but must be re-evidenced against the Zambian national conformity route.
