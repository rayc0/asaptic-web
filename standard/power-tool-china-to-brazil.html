Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Brazil, the mandatory baseline is INMETRO compulsory conformity certification under the applicable Portaria using ABNT NBR IEC 62841 safety testing through an accredited OCP, EMC and electrical safety for the 127/220 V 60 Hz grid, ANATEL homologation for cordless radio modules, restricted-substance and battery handling, and an in-country importer holding a CNPJ; there is no Brazilian equivalent of the EU outdoor-noise directive, so noise is handled through general product-safety and ABNT requirements rather than a stand-alone noise mark.
