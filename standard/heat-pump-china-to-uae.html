Short answer

For UAE, ESMA ECAS conformity marking is the central customs and market entry requirement; mandatory energy label and MEPS apply to air conditioners and heat-pump modes; TRA applies to telecom functions; and GSO IEC 60335-2-40 and related safety frameworks are the main technical baseline. R22 is restricted, 50 Hz has direct compatibility with China, and 230 V single-phase is close to Chinese 220 V within tolerance, while ECAS and GSO requirements remain non-equivalent to CCC.
