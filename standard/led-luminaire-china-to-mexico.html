Short answer

A Chinese LED luminaire manufacturer entering Mexico should plan for Mexican NOM conformity before shipment: screen the luminaire and LED driver against NOM-058-SCFI luminaire safety and NOM-003-SCFI electrical-product safety scopes; certify covered LED lighting products to NOM-031-ENER energy-efficiency requirements through an accredited and approved route; use NYCE, ANCE, or another body only where its approval covers the exact NOM and product scope; prepare Spanish labels, warnings, manuals, importer data, country-of-origin information, and NOM certificate references; and align model-family, certificate-holder, importer, customs, and retail artwork. China CCC, GB 7000.1, China energy-label files, and Chinese-language reports do not replace Mexican NOM certification or Spanish labelling.
