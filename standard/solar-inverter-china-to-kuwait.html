Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Kuwait, the key practical gaps are: (1) confirming whether the exact inverter shipment is in KUCAS scope and needs PAI Technical Evaluation Report and Certificate of Conformity handling before import; (2) obtaining IEC 62109-1/-2 safety type-test evidence rather than relying only on China GB/T 37408; (3) preparing IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 / IEC 62920 grid and EMC evidence for MEW or project-owner review; and (4) validating firmware and protection settings for Kuwait's 240/415 V, 50 Hz electricity system. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 evidence should be treated as useful background only, not as Kuwait/MEW acceptance evidence.
