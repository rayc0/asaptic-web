Short answer

Uzbekistan is an EAEU observer only — TR CU 019/2011 (PPE technical regulation) does NOT apply, and TR EAEU 037/2016 (RoHS-equivalent) does NOT apply. Respiratory PPE must obtain national conformity certification under the Uzstandart (O'zDST) regime, tested against GOST-based standards (GOST 12.4.x series, or adopted EN 149 equivalents). Chinese KN95 (GB 2626) test reports do not directly satisfy O'zDST requirements — separate testing and national certification are required. Labelling must include Uzbek-language information. Goods enter via rail through Kazakhstan, Russia, or China (landlocked country).
