Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Armenia, the baseline is different from non-EAEU CIS markets: Armenia is an EAEU member, so in-scope electrical and electronic products may require EAEU conformity assessment and EAC marking, commonly under TR CU 004/2011 low-voltage equipment and TR CU 020/2011 EMC. That EAEU route sits alongside Armenia-specific SARM standards practice and project-level PSRC / ENA grid-connection requirements. China GB/T 37408-2021, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 evidence can support technical review, but it does not automatically satisfy EAEU TR CU / EAC, SARM, IEC 62109 / 62116 / 61727, or ENA connection gates.
