Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Bosnia and Herzegovina, practical gaps are CE technical-file and declaration work for in-scope EU-aligned legislation, EN/IEC 61851 technical alignment, Type 2 / CCS2 interface migration where required by buyer, operator, or fleet profile, DERK charging-service licensing for public operation, grid project review by NOS BiH, OCPP backend interoperability, and EMC / residual-current protection evidence.
