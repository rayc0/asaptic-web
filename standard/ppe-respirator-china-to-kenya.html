Short answer

Kenya requires respiratory filters and respirators to pass KEBS Pre-Export Verification of Conformity (PVoC), obtain a per-consignment Certificate of Conformity, and carry the Kenya Import Standardization Mark. Chinese GB 2626 test reports and CCC certificates do not replace the Kenya route. For workplace respiratory protection, DOSHS expectations still apply, and the importer must be identified for Mombasa customs clearance. The main practical difference from EU markets is that Kenya uses the KEBS PVoC and import Mark route; for medical masks, PPB controls apply.
