Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Chile, there is no single official whole-unit BESS certificate that should be assumed mandatory for every project. The core gap is scope determination: SEC certification or authorization may apply to listed electrical products and components, Chilean electrical-installation rules apply at site level, UN 38.3 supports lithium-battery transport, and utility-scale projects must satisfy CNE / Coordinador Electrico Nacional connection, operation, and grid-performance requirements.
