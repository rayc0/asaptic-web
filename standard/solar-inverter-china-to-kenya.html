Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Kenya, the practical gaps are KEBS PVoC Certificate of Conformity for imports, model-level safety evidence typically mapped to IEC 62109-1 and IEC 62109-2 or accepted Kenya standards, EPRA and Kenya Power approval for grid-connected projects and net-metering arrangements, and Kenya 50 Hz grid settings. China GB/T or NB/T evidence is useful technical support but should not be treated as a substitute for Kenya import clearance or grid approval.
