Short answer

For Turkey-facing power tools, the practical baseline is CE-based machinery conformity with EN 62841 as the common technical standard family, EN 62841 and EU-reference harmonisation mapped through TSE TS EN where available, LVD and EMC obligations for in-scope accessories, outdoor-noise duties for listed outdoor-use equipment, RoHS substances, and battery-level obligations for cordless tools. Turkish grid practice of 230/400 V 50 Hz differs from China's 220/380 V profile, and in-country importer, representative, and post-market surveillance contact structures are commercially and operationally critical.
