Short answer

For power tools from China entering Thailand, the practical entry gates are TISI applicability, TIS marking for controlled products, NBTC telecom checks for cordless radio functions, and in-country importer/route management via Thailand ports such as Laem Chabang and Bangkok under 230 V 50 Hz operation context. China GB/T or CCC records are technical baselines only and do not replace Thai market-entry conditions.
