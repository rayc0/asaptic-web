Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Mozambique, the practical baseline is INNOQ national conformity against adopted NM/IEC standards (typically the IEC 62841 family for electrical safety), import inspection, INCM type approval for any cordless radio function, and Portuguese-language documentation. Mozambique uses 220 V at 50 Hz; there is no EU-style outdoor-noise product marking regime, so noise is handled as a general safety and documentation topic rather than a standalone mandatory mark.
