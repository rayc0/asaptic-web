Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters to Chile generally need SEC scope clearance and an importer-led evidence chain including in-scope SEC labeling pathway, INN NCh/IEC 62133 safety alignment, SUBTEL radio approvals for wireless functions, and UN 38.3 transport documentation. Chinese GB / GB T standards and CCC records remain China-side evidence and are not a direct substitute for Chilean obligations.
