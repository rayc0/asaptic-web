Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Switzerland, the practical baseline is CE-aligned compliance where Swiss authorities recognize CE via bilateral mutual-recognition, plus Swiss NIN/NEV electrical-rule alignment and Swiss grid review. The practical difference from China is Swiss 230/400 V 50 Hz profile versus China's 220/380 V market baseline for some products, with project-path requirements on fire review, grid interconnection, battery-safety evidence, and local commissioning.
