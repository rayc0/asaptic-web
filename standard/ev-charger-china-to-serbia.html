Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Serbia, the main practical gaps are CE technical-file and declaration work for applicable EU-aligned product legislation, TS EN IEC 61851 technical alignment, Type 2 / CCS2 connector configuration per project deployment and fleet expectation, AERS charging-network licensing for public charging service operations, EMS connection review, OCPP backend interoperability, and EMC / residual-current protection evidence. A blanket whole-unit mandatory national certificate should not be assumed without checking the exact model, voltage, installation route, and use case with Serbian authorities or a qualified local conformity professional.
