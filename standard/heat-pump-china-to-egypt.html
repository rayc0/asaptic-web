Short answer

Obtaining an EOS conformity certificate (with an EGAC-accredited lab test report to ES 6201 / IEC 60335-2-40), affixing the MGEA mandatory energy star-rating label, meeting Egypt's MEPS minimum EER thresholds, and confirming refrigerant compliance under EEAA's HCFC phasedown schedule are the major Egypt compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps. China CCC is not recognised by EOS. Grid compatibility (220 V / 50 Hz) is excellent — no electrical re-engineering is required.
