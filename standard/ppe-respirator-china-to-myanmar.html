Short answer

Myanmar's PPE respirator market is lightly regulated: MSTRD sets MS standards (many adopted from IEC/ISO) but most are voluntary. MIC governs foreign investment and import licensing. Chinese KN95 (GB 2626) respirators can typically enter Myanmar via Yangon/Thilawa port or Mandalay overland from Yunnan with standard customs documentation and product conformity evidence; MSTRD conformity certification may be requested but is not uniformly enforced. Myanmar has no horizontal RoHS equivalent — hazardous substance restrictions do not apply to PPE via a general product regulation. National-language (Myanmar/Burmese script) labelling is expected for consumer-facing products.
