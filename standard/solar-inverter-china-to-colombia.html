Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Colombia, the main gaps are RETIE conformity where the product or installation is in scope, safety evidence commonly mapped to IEC 62109 / NTC IEC 62109, CREG project interconnection rules for distributed and small-scale generation, Ley 1715 renewable-energy project documentation, Colombia's 60 Hz grid settings, and avoiding any assumption that China GB/T reports automatically satisfy Colombian requirements.
