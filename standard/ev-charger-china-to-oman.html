Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Oman, China GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 evidence is useful technical background but should not be treated as automatic Oman acceptance. The main gaps are DGSM scope confirmation, IEC 61851 safety mapping, IEC 62196 connector interoperability, Nama / distributor grid-connection review under APSR-regulated electricity-sector practice, 50 Hz configuration, and the fact that Oman EV policy signals do not by themselves create a single charger type-approval shortcut.
