Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Namibia, the practical gates are: (1) NSI conformity expectations where Namibian standards adopt or reference IEC standards; (2) ECB licensing or regulatory treatment for generation, supply, IPP, and net-metering projects; (3) NamPower or the relevant Regional Electricity Distributor connection approval for grid-tied systems; (4) IEC 62109-1/-2 safety type-test evidence; (5) IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence; and (6) EMC evidence under IEC 61000-series methods and IEC 62920 where required by the project. China GB/T 37408-2021, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 baselines do not automatically satisfy Namibia project acceptance and should be supplemented with IEC evidence and Namibia-specific grid-setting validation for 50 Hz 220/380 V networks.
