Short answer

The main Uganda gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are: mandatory UCC type approval under the Uganda Communications Act 2013 (CE, FCC, and CCC are not recognised by UCC), UNBS IEC-aligned standards for EMC emissions and immunity, electrical safety compliance for Uganda's 240V/50Hz type-G plug environment, appointment of a local Ugandan importer or authorised dealer, URA customs clearance, and advisory alignment with NITA-U cybersecurity guidelines under the Computer Misuse Act 2011.
