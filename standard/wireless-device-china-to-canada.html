Short answer

For a China-made wireless or IoT device entering Canada, the hard gaps are ISED certification to applicable RSS standards such as RSS-247, display of the ISED certification number commonly called the IC number, ICES compliance for digital interference-causing equipment, electrical safety certification through a recognized/SCC-accredited certification body for provincial acceptance, and bilingual English/French consumer labelling. Chinese SRRC, CCC, and domestic labels do not substitute for these Canadian obligations.
