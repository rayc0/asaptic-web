Short answer

Filtering respirators intended for Zambia are not covered by Chinese labels alone. ZABS conformity and import inspection are typically required, the ZABS mark is generally expected, and importer records for the chosen entry corridor (often through Dar es Salaam or Durban into Zambia) must support customs clearance.
