Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Thailand, do not assume a whole-container BESS product certificate is mandatory unless a specific Thai compulsory-standard or project instrument says so. The practical gap is usually component and project diligence: TISI/TIS or IEC battery evidence for cells and batteries, ERC and utility requirements for electricity projects and grid connection, UN 38.3 transport evidence, fire-safety review, EMC evidence, and mapping China GB 44240-2024, GB/T 36276-2023, and GB/T 36558 evidence to Thai project requirements.
