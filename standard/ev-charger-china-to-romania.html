Short answer

For Romania, EV charger market entry requires EU CE marking (LVD/EMC/RED), mandatory IEC 62196 Type 2 (AC) and CCS Combo 2 (DC) connectors per AFIR Regulation 2023/1804, OCPP 1.6J/2.0.1 interoperability, compliance with EN IEC 61851-1/-23 safety standards, ANRE/Transelectrica grid-interconnection documentation, and ASRO standard alignment. The Romanian grid operates at 230/400 V 50 Hz. Romanian-language documentation is expected by ANRE, installers, and local authorities. Constanta is the principal import gateway.
