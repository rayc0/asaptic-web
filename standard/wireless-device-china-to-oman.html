Short answer

The main Oman gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular, or IoT wireless device are: (1) TRA Oman mandatory type approval — the TRA mark and approval number must appear on the device and packaging label before import or sale; (2) EMC testing to GCC GSO / ETSI EN 301 489 series as part of the TRA type approval application — Chinese GB EMC reports are not accepted; (3) electrical safety to IEC 62368-1 under Oman's 230 V / 50 Hz / Type G (BS 1363) mains environment; (4) all imports must be channelled through a licensed Omani distributor or authorized agent — no direct import by end-users or unlicensed entities; and (5) awareness of Oman ITA cybersecurity obligations and Oman PDPPL 2022 data-protection requirements for connected devices and services. Chinese SRRC, CCC, CE, and FCC approvals are not recognised by TRA Oman. The GCC Mutual Recognition Arrangement applies only among GCC member states and does not extend to Chinese approvals.
