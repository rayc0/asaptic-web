Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters to Canada should not rely on CCC, GB 31241, or a CE-style single mark. Canada typically requires TDG/UN 38.3 transport documentation, CCPSA consumer product safety controls, bilingual English/French labelling, and, in many retail channels, certification or listing to Canadian electrical safety standards by an SCC-accredited body such as CSA, UL, or Intertek.
