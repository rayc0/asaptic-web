Short answer

The main Canada gaps for China-origin EV chargers are SCC-accredited electrical approval to Canadian requirements, CSA/UL binational EV charger safety evidence, SAE J1772/CCS1 rather than GB/T 20234 connectors, ISED EMC compliance, provincial Canadian Electrical Code adoption, and English/French consumer-facing documentation.
