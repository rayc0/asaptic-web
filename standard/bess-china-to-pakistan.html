Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Pakistan, do not assume a blanket mandatory whole-unit lithium BESS PSQCA certificate unless the exact HS code, Pakistan Standard, compulsory-item listing, tender, importer route, or authority decision calls it up. The main gaps are confirmation of any PSQCA adoption or acceptance of IEC 62619 / IEC 62933 evidence, UN 38.3 transport documentation, NEPRA and utility grid approval for storage assets, project fire-safety review, and clause mapping from China GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023.
