Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Turkey, the main gaps are CE-aligned conformity for applicable low-voltage, EMC, and radio functions, Turkish adoption or use of EN/IEC safety evidence such as TS EN 62109, EPDK/TEDAS project grid-connection approval, anti-islanding and protection settings, and confirmation that China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 evidence is only a technical baseline, not Turkish market authorization.
