Short answer

For BESS exported from China to the Philippines, do not assume a whole-unit lithium BESS BPS mandatory certification route unless a specific BPS technical regulation, importer route, or project document calls it up. The main gaps are confirmation of any PNS adoption or acceptance of IEC 62619 / IEC 62933 evidence, UN 38.3 transport documentation, ERC / grid approval for storage and ancillary services, Fire Code review, 60 Hz electrical design, and clause mapping from China GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023.
