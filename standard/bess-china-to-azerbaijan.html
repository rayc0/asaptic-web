Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Azerbaijan, the common first gap is domestic Azerbaijan conformity through AZSTAND / AZS national standards and mandatory conformity documentation where in scope, not EAEU EAC certification. Follow-on gaps are IEC-based battery and system safety evidence, Azerenerji or Azerishig grid-connection review, fire-authority approval for the installation, 220/380 V 50 Hz settings, and UN 38.3 transport documentation.
