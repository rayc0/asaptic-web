Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Slovakia, practical gaps are: confirm the applicable ÚNMS route and project-level approval path under ÚRSO oversight; validate that IEC 62109-1/-2 safety testing, IEC 62116 anti-islanding, IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence, and IEC 62920 / IEC 61000 EMC documentation are accepted for Slovakia installations; and prove 230/400 V 50 Hz performance and protection thresholds for grid-connected projects coordinated with SEPS. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 documents are useful but not automatically interchangeable with Slovakia evidence.
