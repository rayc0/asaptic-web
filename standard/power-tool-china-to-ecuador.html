Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Ecuador, the mandatory baseline is conformity with applicable NTE INEN national standards (commonly aligned with IEC 62841) and, where the product is covered by a Reglamento Tecnico Ecuatoriano (RTE), an INEN Certificate of Conformity before customs clearance; cordless tools with radio functions also fall under ARCOTEL homologation, and tools must operate on Ecuador's 120/220 V 60 Hz grid rather than China's 220/380 V 50 Hz.
