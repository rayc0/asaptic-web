Short answer

For China-made household refrigerators entering UAE, the practical gap points are EMC and energy labeling evidence mapping to UAE.S and GSO-referenced baselines, route selection across ECAS and EQM with in-country importer participation, electrical safety evidence under IEC 60335-2-24 with UAE 230/400 V, 50 Hz parameters, and refrigerant handling where R-600a flammability controls are interpreted through UAE safety and environmental controls. Chinese CCC and GB/T/GB evidence is not automatically accepted as UAE conformity evidence.
