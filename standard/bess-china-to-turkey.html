Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Turkey, the practical baseline is CE-aligned product compliance where the equipment falls under Turkish low-voltage, EMC, machinery, radio, RoHS, or related product rules; TSE / TS EN IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence is commonly used for battery and system safety; UN 38.3 is needed for lithium-battery transport; and EPDK plus grid-operator review is separate for storage projects and interconnection.
