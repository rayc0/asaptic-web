Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Bahrain, public sources do not show one universal mandatory whole-product BESS certificate at the import level. The practical compliance route involves BSMD standards screening, IEC 62619:2022 safety evidence for lithium cells and batteries (adopted through GCC via GSO IEC 61427-2:2024 for on-grid storage), UN 38.3 transport documentation, EWA grid-connection review for storage connected to EWA's network, Bahrain Civil Defence fire-safety review for installation, and 50 Hz site configuration. China's GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023 files support technical due diligence but do not replace Bahrain-specific approvals.
