Short answer

For Bangladesh, the main gaps are BSTI certification for covered products, BDS or IEC 60034 evidence, local energy-labelling expectations, 400 V three-phase grid fit, and import documentation. China CCC and GB reports help the technical file but are not Bangladesh approvals.
