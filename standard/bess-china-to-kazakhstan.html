Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Kazakhstan, the common first gap is EAEU EAC conformity for in-scope electrical equipment under TR CU 004/2011 low-voltage safety and TR CU 020/2011 EMC, followed by Kazakhstan project acceptance against ST RK / GOST / IEC battery and system standards, KEGOC or network-owner grid-connection review, fire-safety approval, 50 Hz settings, and UN 38.3 transport documentation.
