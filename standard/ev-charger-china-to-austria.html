For Austria-bound EV chargers, the most critical gaps are Type 2 / CCS2 connector alignment under IEC 62196, compatibility with AFIR obligations as implemented in Austria, CE conformity evidence under LVD / EMC / RED where in scope, RCD / RDC protection, OVE electrotechnical rules, and E-Control / APG installation and grid-operator coordination. 230/400 V 50 Hz parameters are required — voltage differs from China's 220/380 V baseline.
