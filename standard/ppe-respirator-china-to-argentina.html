Short answer

Occupational respirators for worker protection are normally assessed through SRT-referenced workplace safety obligations and IRAM-adopted technical routes, with local importer (CUIT) involvement at Buenos Aires and Zarate. If the model is electrically powered, Argentine entry also requires the S-safety mark process under Secretaria de Comercio safety certification. Medical masks follow ANMAT medical-device pathways. Chinese GB 2626 / GB 19083 marks are not a direct substitute for Argentina mandatory routes.
