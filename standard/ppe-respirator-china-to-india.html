Short answer

India separates non-medical respirator standards and medical-device mask regulation by intended use. IS 9473 is a BIS respirator standard and should be treated as a voluntary presumption route unless a current Indian legal order makes a specific product category compulsory. Medical and surgical masks are regulated by CDSCO under the Medical Device Rules 2017; imported notified medical devices require an Indian Authorized Agent and import licence route. English labelling, importer or authorized-agent details, batch traceability, dates, storage, warnings, and intended-use claims must be India-market specific.
