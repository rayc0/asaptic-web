Short answer

The main Kenya gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are: mandatory CA type approval under the Kenya Information and Communications Act Cap 411A (CE, FCC, and CCC are not recognised by CA), KEBS KS standards for EMC emissions and immunity, electrical safety compliance for Kenya's 240V/50Hz type-G plug environment, a PVOC pre-export verification of conformity certificate obtained from an accredited body (Bureau Veritas/SGS/Intertek) at the Chinese factory before shipment, appointment of a local Kenya importer or authorised dealer, and advisory alignment with CA IoT cybersecurity guidelines.
