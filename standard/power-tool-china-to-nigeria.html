Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Nigeria, the mandatory baseline is SONCAP under the Standards Organisation of Nigeria: a Product Certificate plus a per-shipment SONCAP Certificate are required before customs clearance, with conformity assessed against NIS standards that adopt IEC, typically NIS IEC 62841 for tool safety. Cordless tools with radio functions also require NCC type approval, and an in-country importer is needed.

China domestic noise standards where applicable		 Nigeria does not operate an equivalent of the EU outdoor-noise marking scheme, so there is no mandatory guaranteed sound-power level marking on the tool as a pre-clearance condition under SONCAP for power tools.
