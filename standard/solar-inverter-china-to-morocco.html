Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Morocco, the primary compliance pathway involves IMANOR NM standards that adopt IEC 62109-1 and IEC 62109-2 for inverter safety, ONEE grid-connection requirements under loi 13-09 renewable-energy law, and Morocco's 50 Hz / 220–400 V grid system. Chinese GB/T 37408 and GB/T 19964 are technically aligned in some respects but are not directly recognised; an IMANOR or IEC-route test report and a Morocco-specific ONEE grid settings sheet are needed.
