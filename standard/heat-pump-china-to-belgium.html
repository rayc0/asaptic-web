Short answer

CE marking under LVD, EMC, PED, and Ecodesign directives, Belgian-regional F-gas technician certification, RGIE/AREI electrical installation compliance, and Dutch/French product documentation are the major Belgium-specific compliance requirements for Chinese air-source heat pumps.
