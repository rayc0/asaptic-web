Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Eswatini, the practical gates are: (1) SWASA conformity and any adopted IEC/SADC/SACU standard requirements; (2) ESERA approval or licensing for SSEG and net-metering projects; (3) EEC grid-connection review, commonly expected to align with Southern African NRS 097-2 style interconnection practice; (4) IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence; (5) IEC 62116 / IEC 61727 grid-interconnection evidence; and (6) IEC 61000 / IEC 62920 EMC evidence. China GB/T 37408-2021, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 evidence should not be treated as automatically accepted in Eswatini without supplementary IEC test reports and local utility confirmation.
