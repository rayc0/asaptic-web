Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to The Marshall Islands, the key practical gap is the 60 Hz, 120/240 V grid. Chinese GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 evidence is typically validated for China 220/380 V, 50 Hz conditions and does not by itself demonstrate Marshall Islands grid compatibility. Exporters should plan for 60 Hz firmware, anti-islanding, ride-through, reconnection, protection-threshold, and harmonic revalidation, likely against IEEE 1547 and UL 1741 Americas-style interconnection expectations rather than EN 50549/VDE framing. IEC 62109 safety evidence remains a useful baseline, while Ministry of Works, Infrastructure & Utilities, Ministry of Works, Infrastructure & Utilities, Marshalls Energy Company (MEC), project owner, and insurer requirements should be confirmed for each project.
