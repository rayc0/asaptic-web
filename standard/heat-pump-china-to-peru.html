Short answer

The most critical gap for Chinese heat pumps entering Peru is the 60 Hz grid frequency incompatibility: Chinese 50 Hz compressor and motor designs are not directly compatible and must be redesigned or re-specified. Additional major gaps include INDECOPI conformity declaration against NTP IEC 60335-2-40 (CCC not recognized), MINEM mandatory energy label and MEPS efficiency thresholds, and MINAM ODS permit obligations under Peru's HCFC phase-down schedule.

Electrical Safety — NTP IEC 60335-2-40 and 60 Hz Grid Frequency Compatibility China requires mandatory CCC safety certification for household air conditioners and heat pumps under GB 4706.32 (safety of household and similar electrical appliances — particular requirements for air conditioners), tested at 220 V / 50 Hz. Chinese CCC certificates and GB 4706.32 test reports at 50 Hz are not accepted as evidence of NTP IEC 60335-2-40 compliance in Peru. The critical gap is not only the test standard pathway but the fundamental 60 Hz vs 50 Hz frequency difference that makes Chinese 50 Hz compressor designs physically incompatible with the Peruvian grid.GB 4706.32 (household electrical appliances safety — particular requirements for air conditioners, 50 Hz)
CCC (China Compulsory Certification)
GB 4343.1 (EMC, household electrical appliances)		 Peru's applicable electrical safety standard for heat pumps and air conditioners is NTP IEC 60335-2-40, which adopts IEC 60335-2-40 (safety of household and similar electrical appliances — particular requirements for electrical heat pumps, air conditioners and dehumidifiers). Products must be tested and shown to comply with this standard. A critical hardware consideration is that Peru operates at 220 V / 60 Hz (single-phase residential), whereas China operates at 220 V / 50 Hz. Although the nominal voltage is the same, the 60 Hz frequency makes Chinese 50 Hz compressor motors and drive components NOT directly compatible: compressors, fan motors, and other induction-motor components designed for 50 Hz will run at incorrect speed and reduced efficiency at 60 Hz, and may overheat or fail. Equipment exported to Peru must be designed or re-specified for 60 Hz operation. Safety testing under NTP IEC 60335-2-40 must be performed at the 60 Hz rated conditions.NTP IEC 60335-2-40 — Seguridad de los aparatos electrodomésticos y análogos — Requisitos particulares para bombas de calor eléctricas, aparatos de aire acondicionado y deshumidificadores (adopted from IEC 60335-2-40)

Compressor and motor components must be redesigned or re-sourced for 60 Hz operation before any safety testing is meaningful. Safety testing under NTP IEC 60335-2-40 must be conducted at 60 Hz rated conditions using INDECOPI-recognized accredited laboratories. CCC certificates and 50 Hz test data are not accepted as substitutes.[INFORMATIONAL] Critical gap — Chinese 50 Hz compressor and motor designs are physically incompatible with Peru's 60 Hz grid and must be redesigned; CCC and GB 4706.32 test data at 50 Hz are not accepted; NTP IEC 60335-2-40 testing at 60 Hz is required.
