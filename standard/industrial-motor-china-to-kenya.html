Short answer

Kenya requires KEBS mandatory PVoC (Pre-Export Verification of Conformity) inspection at origin before shipment and the KEBS Diamond Mark of Quality; KS IEC 60034 is adopted for motors; CCC is not recognised. The 415 V / 50 Hz grid differs from China's 380 V standard.
