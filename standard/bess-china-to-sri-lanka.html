Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Sri Lanka, IEC 62619 cell and module safety and IEC 62933 system-level compliance form the primary technical baseline expected by SLSI and project stakeholders. PUCSL and CEB impose grid-connection and storage-integration review requirements. UN 38.3 transport evidence is required where dangerous-goods transport rules apply. Chinese GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023 documentation supports technical due diligence but does not replace Sri Lanka SLSI, PUCSL, or CEB approval processes.
