Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Iceland, the practical baseline is EEA-aligned CE obligations for in-scope electrical, EMC, and radio functions, plus EU Battery Regulation 2023/1542 evidence where battery batteries and lifecycle requirements are triggered. IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 are commonly used technical references, UN 38.3 supports lithium transport, and Landsnet interconnection plus Orkustofnun alignment is handled separately for each site and project class.
