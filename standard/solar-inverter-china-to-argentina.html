Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Argentina, the practical gaps are IRAM / AEA electrical-installation expectations, Argentina product-safety and user-generator documentation where applicable, ENRE / CAMMESA or distributor approval for grid-connected projects, local 50 Hz protection settings, and a clear bridge from China GB/T reports to Argentina project requirements.
