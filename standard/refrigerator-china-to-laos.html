Short answer

For Laos, a China-export refrigerator should be treated as a regulated electrical product requiring local DOSM conformity confirmation through the importer, IEC 60335-2-24 safety evidence, MEM energy-programme checks, and EDL grid-fit review for 230 V / 50 Hz. China’s 50 Hz design is helpful, but the nominal voltage differs from China’s 220/380 V system. Laos has no broad horizontal RoHS regime in force, so RoHS-style EU paperwork should not be presented as a Laos market-access requirement unless a specific tender or buyer contract asks for it.
