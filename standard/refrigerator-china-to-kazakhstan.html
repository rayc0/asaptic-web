Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers face four main Kazakhstan / EAEU compliance gaps: (1) the EAC conformity mark — electrical safety must be assessed under TR CU 004/2011 against GOST IEC 60335-2-24, and EMC under TR CU 020/2011; CCC / GB 4706.13 certification is not accepted; (2) EAC conformity is issued by an EAEU-accredited certification body, with certificate / declaration registration in the EAEU register; (3) EAEU energy-efficiency labelling applies and differs from China's GB 12021.2 grade; (4) an in-country (Kazakhstan / EAEU) importer or applicant established in the Union is required to hold the EAC certificate. The 220/380 V 50 Hz grid matches China, so no voltage / frequency re-engineering is needed, and R-600a is well-positioned but charge documentation against GOST IEC 60335-2-24 must be verified.
