Short answer

The main Fiji gaps for a China-made wireless or IoT device are: (1) TRR (Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Fiji) mandatory type approval for radio and telecom terminal equipment — SRRC and MIIT NAL are not recognised; (2) electrical compliance for 240 V / 50 Hz and Type I (AS/NZS 3112) plug — Chinese CCC electrical approval is not recognised; (3) appointment of a local Fiji authorised importer or agent; (4) English-language labelling and compliance with Fiji Bureau of Standards (FBS/FBOS) references to AS/NZS standards for EMC; and (5) Data Protection Act 2023 obligations where the device processes personal data of Fiji residents. CE, FCC, and CCC marks are not recognised in Fiji.
