Short answer

Georgia is NOT an EAEU member — TR CU 019/2011 does NOT apply. Respiratory PPE (FFP masks/respirators) exported from China to Georgia must comply with GeoSTM national standards (GOST-based, e.g. GOST 12.4.x and adopted EN 149) and require national conformity certification administered by the Georgian National Agency for Standards and Metrology (GeoSTM). Chinese KN95 (GB 2626) test reports do not automatically satisfy Georgian GeoSTM requirements; re-testing to the applicable Georgian/adopted standard is typically required. There is no horizontal RoHS regime in force in Georgia — this topic does not apply to respiratory PPE here. Labelling must include Georgian-language content. Market surveillance is still maturing. Entry via Poti or Batumi (Black Sea).
