Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Oman, the practical compliance gap is usually not a single BESS product mark. Exporters should confirm DGSM or project adoption of IEC battery and BESS standards, prepare IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence where requested, maintain UN 38.3 transport documentation, and map PCS protection, power-quality, fire-safety, and 50 Hz settings to APSR / Nama project requirements.
