Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Colombia, market access centers on product safety to NTC/IEC 62841 (NTC adopting IEC), conformity assessment through ONAC-accredited certification bodies with a Certificate of Conformity, RETIE technical-regulation compliance where the tool is wired into electrical installations, and CRC/ANE radio authorization for cordless or wireless functions. Colombia operates at 110/220 V 60 Hz, differing from China's 220/380 V 50 Hz, and does not have an EU-style horizontal RoHS, battery, or outdoor-noise marking regime.
