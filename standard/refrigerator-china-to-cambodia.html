Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers exporting to Cambodia should address: (1) electrical safety against ISC-adopted CS/IEC 60335-2-24 (Cambodia adopts IEC and ASEAN standards through the Institute of Standards of Cambodia) — CCC/GB 4706.13 certification is not automatically recognised; (2) energy performance under Cambodian energy programmes, which differ in methodology from China's GB 12021.2; (3) conformity and import inspection for regulated products plus an in-country importer of record; and (4) electrical fit for Cambodia's 230 V / 50 Hz grid — the 50 Hz frequency matches China, but the nominal voltage differs from China's 220/380 V. R-600a refrigerant is consistent with regional practice. Cambodia does not operate an EU-style horizontal RoHS, battery, or outdoor-noise regime; those EU-style obligations do not apply.
