Short answer

For BESS exported from China to the UAE, do not assume one blanket mandatory whole-unit ESMA certificate covers every stationary energy-storage project. The defensible starting point is to confirm MoIAT / former ESMA regulated-product scope for the battery, inverter, control, charger, and electrical subassemblies; prepare IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence where specified; complete UN 38.3 for lithium-battery transport; and handle Dubai Civil Defence or other emirate fire-safety and installation review for the site.
