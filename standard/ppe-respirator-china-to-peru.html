Short answer

For respirators sold for worker protection in Peru, importers and manufacturers should align to INACAL-adopted technical references and MTPE occupational use expectations, with conformity handled through the Peru importer workflow rather than China-only certificates. Chinese GB 2626 / NTP / CCC evidence does not automatically transfer. Medical-style masks follow the DIGEMID pathway, and only products with telecom/radio modules face MTC homologation. As in this lane, Peru has no EU-style mandatory horizontal RoHS, battery-regulation, or outdoor-noise regime that replaces this respirator-specific route.
