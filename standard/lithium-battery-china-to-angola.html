Short answer

For China-exported lithium batteries and power banks, Angola market entry is importer-led under IANORQ-focused conformity and customs-inspection expectations, with Portuguese documentation and local import channels. EU-style regimes such as EU RoHS, EU-style battery-passport obligations, and CE marking requirements are not directly equivalent in Angola.
