CROSS-STANDARD · PUBLIC INTEREST

Global Standards Literacy for SMEs — Public-Interest Compliance Index

An open-access, source-linked comparison of Chinese national standards (GB) against destination-market mandatory requirements, to help small and medium exporters understand technical-regulation gaps. Educational resource — not legal, certification, or market-access advice.

English

Asaptic Cross-Standard provides this open-access compliance repository to support small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in navigating the complexities of international technical regulations. Our goal is to enhance standards awareness — a critical pillar for export-led growth and product-quality assurance. This resource is an educational bridge between Greater Bay Area manufacturing capability and global market-entry requirements. It is freely accessible, carries no advertising, and presents official regulator sources per row.
