Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Colombia, do not assume a single whole-unit BESS approval. RETIE is mandatory where the electrical product or installation is in scope, CREG and grid rules matter for connected storage projects, UN 38.3 supports lithium-battery transport, and IEC 62619 / IEC 62933 evidence is normally project, lender, insurer, buyer, or engineering-specification driven unless incorporated into a Colombian requirement.
