Short answer

A Chinese LED luminaire manufacturer entering the Laos market must obtain DOSM mandatory conformity certification for regulated goods; comply with IEC 60598-series and IEC 62560 safety standards as adopted or referenced by DOSM; meet EDL grid requirements at 230 V / 50 Hz (nominal voltage differs from China's 220 V grid); satisfy any MEM energy-efficiency programme requirements for commercial or government procurement; obtain MTPC type approval for wireless-enabled smart luminaires; and note that Laos has no horizontal RoHS law — hazardous-substance restrictions comparable to EU RoHS or CN RoHS do not currently exist in Laos. Transit logistics via Thailand or Vietnam must be factored into documentation and HS code selection.
