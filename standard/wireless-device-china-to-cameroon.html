Short answer

ART mandatory homologation (homologation certificate for all radio/telecom terminal equipment) under Loi n°2010/013 du 21 décembre 2010, ANOR technical standards compliance (NC standards), electrical safety compliance (220 V / 50 Hz, Type C/E French-style plug), bilingual French and English labelling, engagement of a local Cameroonian importer or agent, and DGI/douanes customs processing are the major Cameroon compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC, MIIT network access licence, CCC, and GB-standard documentation. CE and FCC marks are not recognised by ART. CEMAC harmonisation efforts exist but wireless homologation remains nationally enforced by ART. ANTIC provides ICT regulatory oversight but mandatory IoT device security certification is not yet in force.
