Short answer

Vietnam mandates IE2 minimum via QCVN 04:2009/BCT with MOIT energy label; QUATEST conformity testing and a declaration of conformity plus energy certificate are required for import. China GB 18613-2020 IE3 (Grade 3) exceeds the Vietnam IE2 floor, but a separate Vietnam energy label certificate and QUATEST test report are still required.
