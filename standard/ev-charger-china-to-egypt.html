Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Egypt, the practical gaps are EOS / IEC standards mapping, GOEIC import and conformity-document handling, GB/T-to-IEC connector redesign, EgyptERA and utility connection review, electricity-tariff and operating context, and safety / EMC evidence. Do not assume a blanket mandatory whole-unit certificate for every EV charger model without a current EOS / GOEIC / EgyptERA instrument that names the product and route.
