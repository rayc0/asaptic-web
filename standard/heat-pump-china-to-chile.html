Short answer

SEC mandatory product approval (Sello SEC), CNE mandatory energy label and MEPS star-rating compliance, MMA refrigerant and ODS permit obligations (R-22 phasedown), and re-testing or documentation alignment to NCh/IEC 60335-2-40 are the major Chile compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps. Grid compatibility is excellent — Chile operates at 220 V 50 Hz, identical to China. CCC is not recognised by Chilean authorities.
