Short answer

For Chinese air-source heat pumps to Thailand, the major gaps are TISI product certification (with TIS / IEC 60335-2-40 safety basis), mandatory EGAT/MEA P7/5-star energy disclosure, EPPO energy-efficiency policy alignment for EER/COP thresholds, NRCT homologation for wireless-enabled units, and non-recognition of China CCC documentation.
