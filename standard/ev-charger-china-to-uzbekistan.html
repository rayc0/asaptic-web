Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Uzbekistan, China GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 evidence is useful technical documentation but does not by itself close Uzbekistan market-access gaps. Uzbekistan is not an EAEU member — the domestic conformity route is O'zstandart (Uzbek Agency for Technical Regulation), which issues mandatory conformity certificates under O'z DSt / GOST / IEC-harmonised standards for regulated electrical products. The main checks are O'zstandart mandatory conformity assessment, applicable O'z DSt / GOST / IEC 61851 evidence, connector hardware change from GB/T 20234 to IEC 62196 (Type 2 AC / CCS2 DC — physically incompatible), 50 Hz suitability, and project-specific Uzenergo or grid-operator coordination for higher-power installations.
