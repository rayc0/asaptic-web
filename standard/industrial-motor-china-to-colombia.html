Short answer

Colombia is a 60 Hz market. A China-standard 50 Hz, 380 V motor cannot be assumed usable: exporters must redesign or rate the motor for 60 Hz and Colombian voltage, then support NTC/IEC 60034 efficiency, SIC-recognized conformity evidence, and PROURE direction toward IE2 minimum performance.
