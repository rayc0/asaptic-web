Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Mongolia, the practical baseline is MASM conformity certification against applicable MNS standards, commonly GOST or IEC-adopted safety and EMC standards, with Mongolian national-language labelling. Mongolia is not EAEU, so TR CU or TR EAEU certificates do not replace the MASM route, and there is no horizontal EU-style RoHS regime.
