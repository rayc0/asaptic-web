Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Bangladesh, public official sources do not establish a blanket whole-product mandatory BSTI approval for lithium BESS. The practical gap is route confirmation: screen the exact shipment and components for BSTI or importer requirements, keep IEC 62619:2022 / IEC 62933 evidence ready where requested, prepare UN 38.3 transport documents, and obtain project-specific SREDA, utility, fire, and grid-connection review where the BESS is installed or connected.
