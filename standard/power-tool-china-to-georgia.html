Informational comparison of common China power-tool compliance evidence against Georgia GeoSTM market access, national safety and EMC certification, labelling, battery, substances, and workplace-noise expectations.

Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Georgia, treat the route as national GeoSTM conformity assessment using Georgian GOST IEC adoptions of IEC 60745/62841, EMC evidence, Georgian-language labelling, and battery safety evidence for cordless tools. Do not use EAEU TR CU as the target route, and do not invent a fully in-force horizontal RoHS or EU outdoor-noise regime for Georgia.
