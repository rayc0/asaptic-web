Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Nigeria, SONCAP is mandatory where the imported goods are SONCAP-regulated, but a blanket mandatory whole-unit BESS product certificate should not be assumed without confirming the HS code and SON scope. The main gaps are SONCAP Product Certificate / Certificate of Conformity / SONCAP Certificate workflow, SON or project acceptance of IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence, UN 38.3 transport documents, NERC mini-grid or electricity-project approvals for off-grid and C&I storage, fire-safety review, and mapping China GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023 evidence to Nigeria-side requirements.

SONCAP regulated-goods scope check		 SONCAP is Nigeria's pre-shipment verification of conformity process for products imported into Nigeria that are subject to SONCAP. SON states that regulated imports require verification and testing in the country of supply, a Certificate of Conformity from an approved Independent Accredited Firm, and a SONCAP Certificate for Nigerian customs clearance. SON also states that the current regulated scope covers imported goods except listed exclusions, so a BESS shipment should be screened by HS code and product description before assuming exemption or whole-unit mandatory treatment.SONCAP pre-shipment verification of conformity
