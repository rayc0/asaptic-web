Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Nigeria, the main practical gaps are SONCAP import conformity where the goods are regulated, proof against applicable NIS or approved IEC equivalents, IEC 61851 / IEC 62196 safety and connector expectations, NERC or state-regulator grid coordination for connected sites, and EMC evidence. A blanket mandatory whole-unit EV charger certificate was not confirmed from official sources; scope should be verified for the exact HS code, model, charger type, and installation route.
