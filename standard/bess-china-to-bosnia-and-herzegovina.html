Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Bosnia and Herzegovina, the practical baseline is CE-aligned product compliance where the equipment falls under low-voltage, EMC, machinery, radio, RoHS, or related rules; EN/IEC evidence such as IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 is commonly used for battery and system safety; UN 38.3 is needed for lithium-battery transport; and DERK plus NOS BiH grid-project review is separate for storage classification, dispatch, and interconnection.
