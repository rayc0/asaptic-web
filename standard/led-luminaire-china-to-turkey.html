Short answer

A Chinese LED luminaire manufacturer entering the Turkey market must obtain CE marking under the EU-aligned Turkish transpositions of the LVD (TS EN 60598-1), EMC (TS EN 55015 / TS EN 61547), and RoHS directives (Turkey applies these via the EU-Turkey Customs Union); comply with Ecodesign minimum efficacy, CRI, lifetime, and power factor requirements under Turkey's EU-aligned Ecodesign regulation; ensure the EU-aligned Turkish energy-efficiency labelling obligations are met for the light source component; add the EU-aligned A-G energy label and blue light hazard class to packaging where applicable; test the 4 EU-added phthalates not covered by China RoHS; ensure a Turkey-established importer (ithalatçı) is named as the responsible economic operator on the product/packaging; track KKDIK (Turkey's REACH-equivalent) SVHC-type obligations; and supply through ports such as Mersin or Istanbul — none of which are covered by existing CCC certification. The grid is 230/400 V, 50 Hz.
