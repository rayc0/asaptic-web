Short answer

For BESS exported from China to North Macedonia, the practical baseline is CE-aligned product compliance where the equipment falls under low-voltage, EMC, machinery, radio, RoHS, or related product rules; ISRSM / EN battery-safety evidence is commonly required for safety files, UN 38.3 is needed for lithium-battery transport, and ERC plus MEPSO review is separate for storage projects and interconnection.
