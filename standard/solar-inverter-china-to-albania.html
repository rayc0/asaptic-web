Short answer

For China-made solar inverters exported to Albania, the practical gaps are typically in CE-aligned product conformity evidence, TS EN 62109-based electrical safety evidence, inverter firmware settings for 230/400 V 50 Hz, anti-islanding and fault-ride-through parameters, and project-level ERE/OST grid-review alignment. China baseline evidence is technical comparison input, not Albania market authorization.
