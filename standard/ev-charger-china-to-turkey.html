Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Turkey, the main practical gaps are CE technical-file and declaration work for applicable EU-aligned product legislation, TSE / TS EN IEC 61851 technical alignment, GB/T-to-IEC Type 2 or CCS2 connector redesign, EPDK charging-network licensing for public charging service operations, TEDAS and distribution-company grid project review, OCPP backend interoperability, and EMC / residual-current protection evidence. A blanket whole-unit mandatory TSE certificate should not be assumed without checking the exact model, voltage, installation route, and use case with Turkish authorities or a qualified local conformity professional.
