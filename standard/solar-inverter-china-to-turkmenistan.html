Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Turkmenistan, the practical gates are: (1) domestic Turkmenstandartlary conformity assessment, not EAC / TR CU certification; (2) Turkmenenergo project and grid-connection acceptance on the state-controlled 220/380 V, 50 Hz network; (3) IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence; (4) IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence; (5) IEC 61000 / IEC 62920 EMC evidence; and (6) desert heat, dust, ingress-protection, and thermal-derating documentation. China GB/T 37408-2021, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 are useful engineering baselines but are not automatically accepted for Turkmenistan authority or project gates. The nominal grid voltage and frequency match China, but inverter firmware, protection thresholds, reactive-power settings, communications, and environmental derating must still be validated against Turkmenenergo and project-specific conditions.
