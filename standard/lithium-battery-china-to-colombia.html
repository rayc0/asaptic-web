Short answer

Colombia requires on-target evidence tied to RETIE, RETILAP, RETIQ and SIC oversight. For lithium batteries and power banks, the key gap versus China is not only test content but also market architecture: ONAC route certification, importer responsibility, UN 38.3 transport, and entry-document alignment. Colombia has no EU-style horizontal battery regulation, RoHS-style general restriction chapter, or EU-like outdoor-noise framework.
