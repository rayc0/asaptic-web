Short answer

For exports of lithium batteries and power banks to Georgia, GEOSTM currently applies EU-style technical and market surveillance logic in many product paths, plus GNCC checks for wireless. Chinese GB 31241 and CCC export files are useful engineering inputs but do not complete Georgian market placement requirements.
