Short answer

Myanmar entry is mainly an import, conformity-document, grid, radio, and installation acceptance exercise rather than an EU-style CE or RoHS regime. Key gaps for Chinese GB/T chargers are MSTRD documentation, MIC commercial import permits, MPT approval for wireless modules, MEPE or ESE grid acceptance, local 230 V 50 Hz supply assumptions, and connector adaptation for the installed site.
