Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to El Salvador, the practical gaps are OSARTEC / OSN conformity and standards-scope checks, SIGET and distributor interconnection review, IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence, IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence at 60 Hz, IEC 62920 / IEC 61000 EMC evidence, and firmware validation for El Salvador's 120/240 V, 60 Hz grid. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 evidence is based on China 50 Hz grid conditions and should not be treated as automatically accepted in El Salvador.
