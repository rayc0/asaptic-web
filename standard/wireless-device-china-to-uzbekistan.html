Short answer

Wireless devices entering Uzbekistan require UZRTC radio type approval and frequency permit (mandatory for all Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular, and IoT radio equipment) plus a UzStandard Certificate of Conformity (Sertifikat Sootvetstviya) issued by an accredited certification body under O'z DSt / GOST-aligned technical regulations. Uzbekistan is not an EAEU member — the EAC mark does not apply and does not substitute for Uzbek conformity certification. Electrical safety testing is required for the 220 V/50 Hz Type C/F grid. A local authorised importer or agent is mandatory. Chinese CCC, SRRC, FCC, and CE documentation are not recognised and do not replace UZRTC or UzStandard approvals.
