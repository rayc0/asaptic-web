Short answer

For Bulgaria, the practical entry barriers are mainly EU CE conformity execution (LVD 2014/35/EU, EMC 2014/30/EU, RED 2014/53/EU where applicable, plus Battery Regulation (EU) 2023/1542 lifecycle and due-diligence duties), EWRC/ESO grid-interconnection documentation, and BDS/market-acceptance alignment. EU membership makes this a harmonised-framework-first market, with 230/400 V 50 Hz utility conditions and Varna or Burgas (Black Sea ports) as the principal import gateways. Bulgarian-language documentation is often expected by local authorities and installers.
