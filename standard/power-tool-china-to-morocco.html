Short answer

For China-made power tools entering Morocco, the mandatory entry baseline is IMANOR NM-aligned electrical safety and EMC evidence, Ministry of Industry import-conformity attestation with a local importer, ANRT type approval for radio-enabled cordless tools, and workplace safety handling on the user side. The Moroccan public electricity system is 220 V and 50 Hz, with only the frequency matching China directly.
