Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Cameroon, the practical baseline is conformity to ANOR national standards (NC) that adopt IEC 62841, evidenced through the PECAE conformity and import-inspection programme, with French-language documentation; cordless tools with radio functions may additionally need ART approval. There is no EU-style outdoor-noise product marking regime, so noise should be handled honestly as a design and IEC test-method consideration rather than a Cameroon product-marking law.
