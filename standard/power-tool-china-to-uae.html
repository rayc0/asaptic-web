Short answer

Chinese exporters should not assume a China compliance packet is accepted as-is in UAE. Start from MoIAT (ESMA legacy) product scope, secure ECAS/EQM route for applicable categories, align voltage/frequency assumptions to UAE 230/400 V 50 Hz where applicable, and keep local representation and importer accountability documented in the UAE.
