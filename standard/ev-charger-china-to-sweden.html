Short answer

For Sweden-bound EV chargers, the most critical gaps are Type 2/CCS2 connector alignment under IEC 62196, compatibility with AFIR obligations as implemented through Swedish practice (Ei), CE conformity evidence under LVD/EMC/RED where in scope, RCD/RDC protection, cold-climate operation (down to -40 °C is commonly required), and Sweden/Ei/Svenska kraftnät installation and grid-operator coordination including Elsäkerhetsverket electrical-safety rules. 230/400 V 50 Hz parameters are required for project readiness, the Port of Gothenburg is the primary entry port, and Swedish-language documentation is frequently expected.
