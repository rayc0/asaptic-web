Short answer

For China-made lithium batteries and power banks entering the UAE, the confirmed practical gaps are MoIAT/ESMA ECAS scope mapping, in-country importer or registrant involvement, UAE.S and GSO-referenced safety expectations via IEC 62133 and related EMC expectations, and UN 38.3 transport evidence. China GB/CNS/CCC documents are useful engineering support but are not an automatic UAE market-access substitute; model-level gap analysis and destination-side registration and readiness are required before shipping to Jebel Ali or Khalifa.
