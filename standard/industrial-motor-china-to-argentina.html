Short answer

Argentina requires IRAM product certification (or equivalent Certificado de Homologación) for listed motor categories and mandatory energy efficiency labelling aligned to IE classes under the Secretaría de Energía scheme; CCC is not recognised. Grid voltage is 380/220 V 50 Hz — the same as China — so no frequency or voltage redesign gap exists.
