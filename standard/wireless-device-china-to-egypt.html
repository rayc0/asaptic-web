Short answer

NTRA mandatory type approval under Egypt Telecommunications Regulation Law No. 10 of 2003 is the primary gap for any wireless or telecom terminal device exported from China to Egypt. CE mark and CCC are not recognised; SRRC approval does not satisfy NTRA requirements. The NTRA approval number must appear on the product label in Arabic and/or English. Additional gaps include EOS/ES technical and EMC standards conformity, GOEIC import conformity inspection, a local Egyptian importer company, 220 V / 50 Hz electrical safety with Type C/F plug, and Arabic labelling obligations.
