Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering South Korea, the mandatory baseline is KC Safety Certification under the Electrical Appliances and Consumer Products Safety Control Act, KS C IEC 62841 electrical safety, KC-EMC registration via RRA, RoHS-equivalent substance control under the Act on Resource Circulation of EEE and Vehicles, and KC 62133 for cordless lithium battery packs. Korea operates no EU-style outdoor-noise product-marking scheme, so the EU noise directive route does not transfer.
