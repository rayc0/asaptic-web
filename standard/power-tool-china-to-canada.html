Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Canada, the practical baseline is Canadian electrical equipment approval through an SCC-accredited certification body, commonly using the CSA C22.2/UL 62841 binational power-tool standard family, plus ISED ICES EMC compliance for interference-causing electronics, separate battery-pack and charger evidence where applicable, and English/French consumer and safety labelling. China GB 3883 evidence is useful background but does not replace Canadian certification, ISED ICES documentation, or bilingual Canada-market labelling.
