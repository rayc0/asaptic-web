Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Vietnam, no universal whole-unit mandatory BESS certification route is confirmed in this dataset. The main gaps are project or tender evidence for TCVN / IEC 62619 and IEC 62933, UN 38.3 lithium-battery transport testing, MOIT / EVN grid-connection review, fire-safety installation review, and EMC evidence for power-conversion and electronic equipment where applicable.
