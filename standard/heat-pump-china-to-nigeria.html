Short answer

SON MANCAP mandatory product certification (including pre-shipment inspection) to NIS/IEC 60335-2-40 safety standards, energy efficiency labelling under the NEEAP/NERC/ECN programme, R-22 phasedown compliance under Nigeria's HCFC programme, and voltage adaptation for 240 V 50 Hz supply are the major Nigeria compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pump exporters. China's CCC certification is not recognised by SON.
