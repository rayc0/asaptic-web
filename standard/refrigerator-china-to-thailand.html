Short answer

For China-made refrigerators, Chinese CCC and GB/T reports are technical inputs only and are not destination-compliant by themselves for Thailand. Thai placement commonly requires an in-country importer licence-holder process, TISI route confirmation for controlled categories, Thai 230 V 50 Hz adaptation, and EGAT/DEDE energy-label compliance for applicable models before distribution through Laem Chabang or Bangkok channels.
