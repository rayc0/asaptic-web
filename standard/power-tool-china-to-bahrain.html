Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Bahrain, the practical baseline is national conformity registration administered by BSMD under the Ministry of Industry and Commerce, using GSO standards that adopt the IEC 62841 series for electrical safety and the relevant IEC/CISPR electromagnetic compatibility standards; cordless tools with a wireless radio module additionally need TRA type approval. Bahrain operates on 230 V, 50 Hz.
