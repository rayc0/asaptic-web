Short answer

QCVN mandatory conformity declaration (MOIT), mandatory energy labelling (MOIT star-rating programme), MONRE ODS/refrigerant controls with R-22 phase-down and preference for R-32/R-410A, TCVN/IEC 60335-2-40 safety testing via STAMEQ/QUATEST, and the non-recognition of Chinese CCC certification are the major Vietnam compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps. 50 Hz grid frequency and 220/380 V voltage match China, so no electrical frequency or voltage adaptation is required.
