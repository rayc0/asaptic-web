Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Moldova, the practical baseline is CE/EN-aligned product documentation plus Type 2 / CCS2 interoperability planning for the target model, plus ANRE and Moldelectrica site-level grid and service review. Moldova is 50 Hz like China but uses 230/400 V nominal voltage, so charger settings and protections should be confirmed against site design. Because Moldova is landlocked, project logistics usually involve Romanian transit and Giurgiulești corridor planning.
