Short answer

CE marking (mandatory for all EU members including Poland), Ecodesign SCOP thresholds tested to EN 14511/EN 14825, F-gas compliance under EU Regulation 2024/573 plus Polish F-gas Act registration with KOBiZE/CRO, UDT pressure equipment inspection under PED 2014/68/EU, Polish-language documentation for end users, and eligibility for Czyste Powietrze subsidy are the major compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps entering Poland.
