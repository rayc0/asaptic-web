Short answer

A Chinese LED luminaire or LED lamp manufacturer entering India must first confirm whether the exact product is notified under the Electronics and IT Goods (Requirements for Compulsory Registration) framework administered through BIS CRS. Notified LED luminaires are commonly assessed against IS 10322 series requirements and notified self-ballasted LED lamps against IS 16102 series requirements; CRS registration and Standard Mark use are required before sale for in-scope goods. BEE star labelling may also apply to LED lamps under the Bureau of Energy Efficiency programme. EMC evidence should be checked against applicable Indian Standards and CISPR-based lighting EMC references. Indian Standards should be treated as voluntary technical routes that may support presumption or evidence of conformity; the binding obligation comes from the Act, Rules, Orders, and official notifications.
