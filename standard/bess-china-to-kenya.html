Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Kenya, the clearest mandatory gate is KEBS import conformity: products subject to PVoC normally need a Certificate of Conformity against applicable Kenya standards or approved specifications before shipment. IEC 62619, IEC 62933, UN 38.3, EPRA licensing / mini-grid review, and fire / EHS evidence are usually triggered by product scope, project design, grid connection, transport route, contract, insurer, or authority review rather than one universal BESS certificate.
