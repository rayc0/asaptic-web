Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Vietnam, the main practical gaps are TCVN / IEC 61851 evidence, GB/T 20234-to-Type 2 or CCS2 connector redesign where the project specifies IEC connectors, MOIT and EVN charging-station and grid processes, OCPP backend interoperability where required by the operator, and IEC-family EMC / RCD safety evidence. Do not assume a blanket mandatory whole-unit Vietnam charger certificate without checking the exact charger type, installation route, and current regulator list.
