Short answer

The main Australia gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are ACMA radiocommunications compliance under the Radiocommunications Equipment (General) Rules 2021, ACMA EMC compliance, telecommunications customer equipment labelling where a network interface is present, RCM labelling, EESS electrical safety where the product is in-scope electrical equipment, and registration by an Australian responsible supplier. AS/NZS 4268, CISPR 32 / AS/NZS CISPR 32, and related AS/NZS standards are voluntary presumption or evidence routes, not the mandatory law themselves.
