Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Egypt, no blanket whole-unit mandatory BESS certification has been confirmed from the cited public sources. The practical compliance file should instead be split by route: GOEIC / EOS import conformity for electric batteries or listed components where applicable, IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence where requested by customers or authorities, UN 38.3 transport documentation, EgyptERA grid-connection review, and project-specific fire-safety and EMC evidence.
