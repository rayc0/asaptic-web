Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Senegal, the practical gaps are ASN or project-required standard evidence, IEC 62109-1 and IEC 62109-2 safety documentation where requested, CRSE authorization or regulatory classification for generation projects, Senelec connection acceptance for grid-tied systems, and Senegal 50 Hz site settings. China GB/T or NB/T evidence is useful technical support but should not be treated as a substitute for Senegal regulatory or grid approval.
