Short answer

ARTCI mandatory homologation (type approval certificate) under Loi 2012-293 sur les Télécommunications/TIC — with French-language technical documentation and test reports from ARTCI-recognised or ILAC/MRA-accredited labs — an authorised Ivorian importer or représentant local, CNI-aligned EMC compliance (including the 5 GHz indoor use restriction), and 220 V / 50 Hz Type C/E electrical safety are the major Côte d'Ivoire compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC, MIIT NAL, CCC, and GB-standard documentation. CE and FCC marks are not directly recognised by ARTCI. ECOWAS (CEDEAO) harmonised type approval is developing but ARTCI national homologation remains mandatory.
