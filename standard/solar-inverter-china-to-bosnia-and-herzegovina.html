Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Bosnia and Herzegovina, main gaps are CE-aligned conformity for applicable low-voltage, EMC, and radio functions, Bosnia market evidence of EN/IEC safety such as EN IEC 62109, DERK/NOS BiH project grid-connection review, anti-islanding and protection settings, and confirmation that China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 evidence is only technical baseline, not Bosnia market authorisation.
