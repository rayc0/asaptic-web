Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Paraguay, the practical gaps are INTN conformity expectations using IEC-family evidence, ANDE project-level technical approval for grid-connected installations, connector conversion from GB/T 20234 to IEC 62196 Type 2 for AC and CCS2 for DC, IEC 61851 safety evidence, IEC 61000 EMC and power-quality evidence, and OCPP or project-specified back-office integration for networked chargers. Paraguay operates on 220/380 V 50 Hz, nominally aligned with China, but ANDE connection terms, protection settings, metering, load management, Spanish documentation, and site commissioning still need project confirmation. A standalone Paraguay EVSE product certification rule could not be confirmed from official sources as of 2026-06-14; exporters should verify the current INTN route, HS code, ANDE approval path, charger type, and project-owner specification before shipment.
