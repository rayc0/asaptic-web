Short answer

Chinese cosmetics exporters can generally sell cosmetics in Canada without pre-market approval, but the product must be safe, comply with the Food and Drugs Act and Cosmetic Regulations, meet Hotlist ingredient restrictions, be manufactured and stored under sanitary conditions, carry required English/French labelling with INCI ingredients, and be notified to Health Canada through the CNF within 10 days after first sale.
