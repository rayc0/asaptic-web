Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Bangladesh, the practical gaps are BSTI scope confirmation, IEC 62109-1 and IEC 62109-2 safety evidence where requested, SREDA net-metering documentation for rooftop or customer-side solar, BPDB / DPDC or other utility grid-connection acceptance, and Bangladesh 50 Hz protection settings. China GB/T evidence is useful technical support but should not be treated as a substitute for Bangladesh scope, grid, project, or customs acceptance.
