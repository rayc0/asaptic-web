Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Israel, the key practical gaps are: (1) confirming whether the inverter is within an SII mandatory Israeli Standard or import-control route; (2) obtaining model-specific SI / IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence instead of relying only on China GB/T 37408 or NB/T 32004 files; (3) documenting IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 grid-interface compliance for Israel Electricity Authority and utility connection review; (4) supplying IEC 62920 / IEC 61000 EMC evidence; and (5) validating inverter firmware and protection settings for Israel's 230/400 V, 50 Hz grid. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 evidence should be treated as China-side baseline evidence only, not as Israel/SII approval evidence.
