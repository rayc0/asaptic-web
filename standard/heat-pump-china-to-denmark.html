Short answer

CE marking (LVD, EMC Directive, PED), EU F-gas Regulation (EU) 2024/573 with KMO technician certification, Ecodesign SCOP thresholds (EN 14511/EN 14825), Danish-language documentation, and cold-climate performance validation are the major Denmark compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps.
