Short answer

For covered three-phase industrial AC motors, Singapore market access turns on EMA MEPS and registration, SS or IEC 60034 technical evidence, local responsible-supplier control, 400 V 50 Hz suitability, and IMDA review where drives or radio functions are included. China GB and CCC evidence is useful background but is not recognized as Singapore approval.
