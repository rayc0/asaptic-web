Short answer

Chinese respirator files such as GB 2626 and GB/T accessories evidence are useful technical references but do not automatically satisfy Thailand market controls. Key Thailand gates are TISI compliance interpretation, Ministry of Labour occupational safety obligations, and importer-linked placement in Thailand. For powered accessories or telecom-enabled functions, NBTC and EGAT/DEDE requirements are separately checked. Main electrical context is 230 V 50 Hz.
