Short answer

INACOM mandatory homologação (type approval) under Lei 23/11, an authorized Angolan importer or local agent, Portuguese-language documentation, electrical safety compliance (220-230 V / 50 Hz, Type C plug), IANORQ conformity assessment, and cybersecurity obligations under Lei 7/17 are the major Angola compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC, MIIT network access licence, CCC, and GB-standard documentation. CE, FCC, and CCC marks are not recognized by INACOM.
