Short answer

For Chinese air-source heat pumps entering South Africa, the major compliance gaps are the NRCS mandatory Letter of Authority under VC 8055 (CCC not recognised), SANEDI mandatory energy label and MEPS under the 5-star scheme (Energy Efficiency Regulations), SANS 60335-2-40 electrical safety (230 V / 50 Hz grid; load-shedding voltage tolerance), DFFE ODS permit for R-22 or controlled refrigerants, and ICASA type-approval for any wireless-enabled unit.
