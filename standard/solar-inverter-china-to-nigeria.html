Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Nigeria, the main gaps are SONCAP Product Certificate / Certificate of Conformity / SONCAP Certificate steps for regulated imports, SON product certification or registration where applicable, IEC 62109 safety evidence as an approved-equivalent route rather than an automatic whole-unit approval, NERC grid-code or mini-grid project acceptance, Nigeria 50 Hz settings, EMC evidence, and proof that China GB/T reports are only supporting baseline documents.
