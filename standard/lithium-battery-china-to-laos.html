Short answer

Chinese GB 31241 or CCC evidence is only a starting point for Laos. Exporters should confirm whether the exact lithium battery, power bank, charger, or wireless-enabled product is inside DOSM regulated-goods scope, prepare IEC 62133/ASEAN-adopted safety evidence, use MPT approval for wireless functions where applicable, and retain UN 38.3 transport documentation for landlocked transit via Thailand or Vietnam.
