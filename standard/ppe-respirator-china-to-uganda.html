Short answer

Uganda requires UNBS-aligned conformity evidence through PVoC and a UNBS Certificate of Conformity, plus importer-linked readiness and Q-Mark controls for regulated routes. Chinese GB 2626 and GB 19083 documents are a domestic starting point only and do not automatically satisfy Uganda import clearance. Occupational respirators must follow EN 149 class requirements as adopted by UNBS, while medical-mask claims remain a separate national health pathway.
