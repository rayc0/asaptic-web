Short answer

For a China-made Wi-Fi or Bluetooth IoT device entering Vanuatu: mandatory TRR type approval is required under the Telecommunications and Radiocommunications Regulation Act 2009 for radio and telecom terminal equipment; AS/NZS or ETSI/CE-basis test reports may be used as technical evidence for TRR submission but CE, FCC, and CCC marks are not independently recognised as substitutes for TRR approval; Vanuatu uses 220–240 V / 50 Hz with both Type I (Australian) and Type C (French) plugs in active use; a local Vanuatu importer is required; no mandatory pre-market cybersecurity certification exists as of 2026; and bilingual English/French product documentation is required reflecting Vanuatu's official language policy.
