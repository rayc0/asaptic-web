Short answer

PSQCA mandatory PS Mark pre-market approval, NEECA mandatory energy label (1-5 stars) and minimum energy efficiency standards, PTA obligations for wireless-enabled units, and ODS refrigerant phase-down requirements are the major Pakistan compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps. China CCC is not recognised by Pakistan; fresh PSQCA pre-market approval is required before customs clearance.
