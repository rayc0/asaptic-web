Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters must check whether the product contains button/coin cells covered by ACCC mandatory standards, appoint or work through an Australian Responsible Supplier for in-scope electrical equipment, support RCM/EESS compliance, keep AS/NZS IEC 62133 evidence where applicable, and provide UN 38.3 plus ADG dangerous-goods transport documentation; Chinese GB 31241 or CCC evidence alone does not satisfy Australian market obligations.
