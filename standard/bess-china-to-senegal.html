Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Senegal, the public sources reviewed did not identify a single Senegal product certificate dedicated to stationary lithium BESS. The practical gap is usually project-level: ASN or customer acceptance of IEC 62619 / IEC 62933 evidence, CRSE / Senelec electricity-sector and interconnection review for storage projects, local fire-safety review, 50 Hz site settings, and UN 38.3 transport evidence. China GB 44240-2024 and GB/T 36276-2023 are useful baselines but are not Senegal approvals by themselves.
