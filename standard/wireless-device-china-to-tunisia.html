Short answer

INT (Instance Nationale des Télécommunications) mandatory homologation under the Code des Télécommunications (Loi 2001-1 modifiée) is the primary gap for any wireless or telecom terminal device exported from China to Tunisia. CE mark, CCC, and SRRC approval are not recognised by INT; a separate Tunisian homologation process is required. Additional gaps include INNORPI/NT-aligned EMC standards (ETSI/IEC-referenced), 230 V / 50 Hz electrical safety with Type C/E plug, bilingual Arabic and French product labelling, a local authorised importer or agent, and Direction Générale des Douanes customs clearance.
