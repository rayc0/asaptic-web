Short answer

Exporting BESS from China to Luxembourg requires CE marking under LVD 2014/35/EU and EMC Directive 2014/30/EU, full compliance with EU Battery Regulation 2023/1542 (including battery passport from 18 Feb 2027), and grid connection approval from Creos under EN 50549-1/-2 and Commission Regulation (EU) 2016/631. Chinese standards (GB/T 36276-2023, GB/T 34120-2023, GB/T 36558-2023) are not accepted as EU equivalents. Key gaps: thermal runaway propagation testing to IEC 62933-5-2/UL 9540A, EN 50549 grid code compliance (230/400 V 50 Hz vs China 220/380 V), and ADR 2025 transport documentation. Luxembourg's regulatory framework is administered by ILNAS (standardisation), ILR (energy regulation), and Creos (grid operator). French and German language documentation is often expected.
