Short answer

Chinese GB 31241 or CCC evidence is only a starting point for Myanmar. Exporters should confirm whether the exact lithium battery, power bank, charger, or wireless-enabled product is inside MSTRD conformity scope, prepare MS/IEC 62133 safety evidence, use MPT approval for embedded wireless functions where applicable, and retain UN 38.3 transport documentation for Yangon/Thilawa seaport or Mandalay overland routes from Yunnan. Myanmar does not have a horizontal RoHS regime.
