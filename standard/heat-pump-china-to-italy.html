Short answer

CE marking (LVD + EMC + PED), F-gas certification including Italian DPR 146/2018 patentino for technicians, Ecodesign SCOP thresholds (EN 14511/EN 14825), and registration in the Italian FGAS registry are the major Italy-specific compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps, on top of the standard EU framework.
