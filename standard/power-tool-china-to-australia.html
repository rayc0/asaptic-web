Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Australia, the mandatory baseline is Australian electrical safety law as applied through EESS/RCM for in-scope equipment, ACMA EMC compliance for regulated electrical products, and battery or charger obligations where applicable; AS/NZS 62841 and related AS/NZS standards are voluntary evidence routes and should not be described as mandatory standards.
