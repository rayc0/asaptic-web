Exporting Wireless Devices from China to Bhutan — BICMA Type Approval & BSB Requirements

Regulatory requirements for wireless and telecom terminal equipment sold or imported into Bhutan, including BICMA type approval, BSB electrical safety, local importer rules, and the Bhutan Information, Communications and Media Act 2018.

Short answer

Wireless devices exported from China to Bhutan require a separate BICMA type approval — CE, FCC, and CCC marks are not recognised. A Bhutanese authorised importer (typically Thimphu-based) must be appointed before importation. BSB electrical safety and EMC testing apply, and the Bhutan Information, Communications and Media Act 2018 governs cybersecurity obligations.
