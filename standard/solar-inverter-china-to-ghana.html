Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Ghana, GSA conformity assessment is required for electrical energy equipment at import, and IEC 62109-1/-2 is the internationally referenced safety standard. Energy Commission LI 2461 (2022) establishes inverter standards and labelling requirements. ECG and NEDCo grid connection follows the Net Metering Code 2023 and requires inverters to operate at 50 Hz with anti-islanding protection and power factor ≥ 0.98. Chinese GB/T 37408 and GB/T 19964 cover overlapping technical ground but are not accepted as substitutes for Ghana conformity documents.
