Short answer

For China-made BESS entering New Zealand, the main gaps are RCM / EESS evidence under the Electricity (Safety) Regulations 2010, IEC 62619 / IEC 62133 / IEC 62040 product-safety reports, AS/NZS 5139:2019 and AS/NZS 3000 installation compliance, AS/NZS 4777.2 inverter settings for New Zealand, Fire and Emergency New Zealand / building-consent review where applicable, local distributor approval, and UN 38.3 transport evidence. Chinese GB/T 36558-2023, GB/T 34120-2023, and NB/T 42090-2016 reports are not accepted as New Zealand approvals.
