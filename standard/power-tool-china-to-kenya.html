Short answer

For China-made power tools entering Kenya, the mandatory baseline is KEBS PVoC import conformity for regulated models, a Certificate of Conformity, and the Standardization/Import Standardization Mark at clear-in process. Power-tool body safety is typically aligned to KS IEC 62841 as a Kenya-adopted technical basis. Cordless models with radio functionality need Communications Authority approvals in addition to electrical safety evidence. Kenya uses 240 V, 50 Hz, matching frequency but not nominal voltage profile from China (220/380 V), and no EU-style horizontal noise-marking, RoHS, or battery-regime mark is confirmed as a single mandatory product-level gate.
