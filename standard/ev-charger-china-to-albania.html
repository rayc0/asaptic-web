Short answer

For China-made EV chargers exported to Albania, the practical gaps are CE-aligned product and EMC readiness, TS EN IEC 61851 electrical-safety evidence, Type 2 / CCS2 connector and locking compatibility, OCPP backend acceptance, ERE / OST site-grid coordination, and EPDK-style service-licensing equivalents where public operation is involved. GB/T-only configurations are a technical baseline only and require converter planning for Albanian deployment.
