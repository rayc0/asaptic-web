Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Bangladesh, do not assume that China GB/T 18487 or GB/T 20234 evidence directly closes the Bangladesh file. The practical gaps are confirming whether BSTI has an applicable Bangladesh Standard or compulsory import / certification route for the exact charger, mapping safety evidence to IEC 61851 where requested, resolving GB/T 20234 connector incompatibility against IEC 62196 project expectations, and obtaining BPDB or distribution-utility review for grid connection, metering, protection, power quality, and site energisation. Bangladesh and China both use 50 Hz, but the exact voltage, earthing, utility protection settings, and site approval still need local validation.
