Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Argentina, there is no single universal Argentina BESS product certificate that automatically replaces project due diligence. The main gaps are IRAM / IEC evidence requested by buyers or engineers, UN 38.3 lithium-battery transport documentation, CAMMESA and local grid-connection studies for SADI projects, site fire-safety approval, 50 Hz equipment suitability, and a clause-by-clause crosswalk from China GB 44240-2024 / GB/T 36276-2023 to the Argentina project route.
