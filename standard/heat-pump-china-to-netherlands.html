Short answer

F-gas certification (EU Regulation 2024/573) with Dutch STEK technician requirements, Ecodesign SCOP thresholds (EN 14511/EN 14825) and RVO ISDE positive-list registration, Pressure Equipment Directive classification, CE marking under LVD and EMC directives, and Dutch-language end-user documentation are the major compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps entering the Netherlands.
