Short answer

FFP filtering facepiece respirators entering Ghana fall under the Ghana Standards Authority (GSA) mandatory conformity-assessment regime, with destination inspection and the G-Mark scheme. Ghana Standards (GS) commonly adopt IEC/EN standards, so the practical performance benchmark is GS/EN 149 (FFP1/FFP2/FFP3). Chinese KN95 (GB 2626) test reports do not automatically satisfy the GSA-adopted EN 149 route. A locally established importer is required to clear goods at Tema or Takoradi, and the Labour Department occupational-safety framework governs workplace use. Grid note: Ghana is 230 V / 50 Hz (50 Hz matches China; voltage differs from China 220/380 V).
