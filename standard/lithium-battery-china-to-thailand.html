Short answer

Major gaps are Thailand mandatory TIS conformity pathways for controlled battery products, mandatory or function-linked NBTC requirements for radio features, and country-specific importer/licence and port-clearance workflow. UN 38.3 remains the core lithium transport baseline and requires evidence alignment to Thailand-facing logistics.
