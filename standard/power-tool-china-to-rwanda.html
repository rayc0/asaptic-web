Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Rwanda, the practical baseline is RSB Pre-Export Verification of Conformity (PVoC) with a Certificate of Conformity before shipment, conformity to RS standards that adopt the relevant IEC electrical-safety series (RS/IEC 62841), and an S-Mark where required; cordless tools with wireless functions may also fall under RURA radio authorisation, and Rwanda is landlocked so goods route via Mombasa or Dar es Salaam.
