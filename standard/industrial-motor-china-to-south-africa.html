Short answer

The primary gate for China-to-South-Africa industrial motors is the NRCS Letter of Authority (LoA), required before import or sale of motors covered by a compulsory specification; SABS SANS 60034 (IEC 60034 adoption) sets the technical standard, and MEPS IE2 direction applies under DoE energy policy. CCC is not recognised.
