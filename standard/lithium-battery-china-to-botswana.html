Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters targeting Botswana should plan for BOBS conformity assessment and the BOS import mark where the product is in scope, IEC 62133 cell-safety evidence, electrical-safety testing against BOS-adopted IEC/SANS standards, BOCRA type approval for any wireless feature, UN 38.3 transport testing, and an in-country importer — none of which is satisfied by Chinese GB 31241 or CCC certification alone. Botswana has no EU-style horizontal battery regulation or RoHS regime.

Lithium Battery Transport — UN 38.3 and Dangerous Goods China applies the same UN 38.3 basis: lithium batteries for export are tested to UN 38.3 and shipped under GB 19521 / GB-aligned dangerous-goods rules, with the air mode governed by CAAC rules aligned to IATA DGR.
