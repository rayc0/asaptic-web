Short answer

The main South Africa gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are mandatory ICASA type approval under the Electronic Communications Act No. 36 of 2005 (CE and FCC not recognised), SABS/SANS EMC compliance (SANS 55032 / SANS 55035), NRCS Letter of Authority for in-scope electrical products including power adapters, appointment of a local South African importer or authorised dealer for ICASA registration, and advisory alignment with the South Africa Cybersecurity Policy Framework. CCC is not recognised in South Africa.
