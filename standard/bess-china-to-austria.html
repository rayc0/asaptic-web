Short answer

For Austria, the practical entry barriers are mainly EU CE conformity execution (LVD 2014/35/EU, EMC 2014/30/EU, RED 2014/53/EU where applicable, plus Battery Regulation (EU) 2023/1542 lifecycle and due-diligence duties), E-Control / APG grid-interconnection documentation, and ASI / market-acceptance alignment. Austria is a landlocked EU member state; sea freight routes via Hamburg, Koper, or Trieste. OVE (Österreichischer Verband für Elektrotechnik) electrotechnical rules apply alongside EU directives, and German-language documentation is often expected. The grid is 230/400 V 50 Hz — voltage differs from China's 220/380 V baseline.
