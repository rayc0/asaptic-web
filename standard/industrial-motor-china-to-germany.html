Short answer

For Germany, the decisive gaps are EU Regulation 2019/1781 IE3/IE4 efficiency compliance, CE marking with EU DoC under LVD and EMC, an EU-based authorised representative or responsible economic operator, and readiness for BNetzA market surveillance. CCC and China GB compliance are not recognized as German or EU market approval.
