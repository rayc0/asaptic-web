Short answer

Respiratory PPE (KN95/respirators) exported from China to Mongolia requires national conformity certification via MASM (Mongolian Agency for Standardization and Metrology) based on MNS standards that adopt GOST 12.4.x or EN 149 equivalents. Mongolia is NOT an EAEU member — TR CU 019/2011 (the EAEU PPE technical regulation) does not apply. There is no horizontal RoHS regime. Goods transit via China (Tianjin port) or Russia. Mongolian-language labelling is required. Chinese GB 2626 (KN95) test reports may partially support MASM certification if the applicable MNS standard closely mirrors GOST, but exporters must confirm the current MNS standard and MASM certification scope before shipment.
