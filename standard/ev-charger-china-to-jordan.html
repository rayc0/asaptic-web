Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Jordan, the main confirmed gaps are Jordan-side JSMO standards and EV approval checks, EMRC construction permit and operating licence routes for public charging stations, IEC 61851 safety and EMC evidence, connector conversion from GB/T 20234 to the IEC 62196 interface expected by the target network or imported vehicles, and utility grid-connection acceptance under Jordan's 50 Hz electricity system. A blanket JSMO whole-unit mandatory EVSE certificate could not be confirmed from official public sources as of 2026-06-14, so exporters should verify the exact product scope, site licence route, and utility connection requirements before shipment.
