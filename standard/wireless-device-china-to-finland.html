Short answer

Finland fully applies the EU Radio Equipment Directive (RED 2014/53/EU); CE marking under RED is the primary gate enforced by Traficom. Key gaps versus Chinese SRRC/CCC include: radio performance testing to EN 300 328 / EN 301 893 (with mandatory 5 GHz DFS/LBT — Traficom actively enforces spectrum compliance), EMC to EN 301 489 series, electrical safety to EN IEC 62368-1:2020+A11 (230 V/50 Hz, plug type C/F Schuko), cybersecurity under RED Art. 3.3(d)–(f) via EN 18031 (mandatory from 1 August 2025; Finland is a high-connectivity EU market with one of the world's highest mobile data usage rates, making CRA 2027 compliance a near-term priority), EU Authorised Representative, bilingual Finnish and Swedish language labelling for consumer products (Finland is constitutionally bilingual), and WEEE producer registration in Finland via Elker ry or PYR Oy. CCC and FCC are not recognised.
