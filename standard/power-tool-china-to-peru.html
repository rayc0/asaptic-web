Short answer

For China-made power tools entering Peru, the mandatory baseline is: INACAL-adopted NTP safety references where a PRODUCE/INDECOPI Reglamento Tecnico makes them mandatory, MTC equipment homologation for wireless cordless models, in-country importer-led customs and post-market accountability at Callao, and Spanish labeling/consumer information under INDECOPI. Peru does not apply an EU-style CE, no EU-style standalone EMC mark for non-radio tools, no EU-style outdoor-noise label, no EU-style RoHS regime, and no EU-style battery passport regime.
