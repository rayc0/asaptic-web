Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Ghana, the practical baseline is GSA conformity assessment and destination inspection (G-Mark / conformity certificate), product safety to GS standards that adopt IEC/EN 62841, and NCA type approval for the radio module in cordless tools. There is no EU-style outdoor-noise product marking regime; noise is handled through occupational and general safety frameworks rather than a dedicated product directive.
