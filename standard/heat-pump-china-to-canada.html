Short answer

NRCan minimum performance and EnerGuide filing, CSA/SCC electrical safety certification to Canadian standards such as CSA C22.2 No. 236 and CSA C22.2 No. 120, federal HFC controls, and English/French bilingual markings and documents are the major Canada compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps.
