Short answer

For power tools from China to Indonesia, the high-friction points are Indonesia’s mandatory SNI pathway based on IEC 62841 adoption, LSPro/SPPT-SNI mark readiness, and in-country importer responsibilities, while cordless products with radio features must also satisfy SDPPI approval. Chinese GB-based safety files are useful, but they do not complete SPPT-SNI, importer-side responsibility, or Indonesian market-facing documentation on their own.
