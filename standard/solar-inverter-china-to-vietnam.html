Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Vietnam, the main gaps are Vietnam TCVN / IEC 62109 safety evidence requested by projects or buyers, MOIT and EVN grid-connection review including anti-islanding evidence, rooftop-solar eligibility under current self-consumption and DPPA rules, 50 Hz grid re-validation against Vietnam settings, and EMC or radio-module compliance where the product design is in scope.
