Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Fiji, the key practical gaps are: (1) confirm the applicable DNTMS standards or conformity route and any FCCC consumer-product obligations before import; (2) provide IEC 62109-1/-2 safety type-test evidence for the exact inverter model or family; (3) provide IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence for EFL grid-connection review; (4) provide IEC 62920 and IEC 61000-series EMC evidence; (5) validate firmware and protection settings for Fiji's 240/415 V, 50 Hz network. Fiji has the same 50 Hz frequency as China, but the nominal voltage differs from China's 220/380 V baseline. Cyclone exposure, high humidity, salt-mist corrosion, and thermal derating should be addressed in the project technical file.
