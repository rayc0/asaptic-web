Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Egypt, do not assume a China GB/T or IEC report alone is enough. The practical package should confirm EOS/Egyptian Standard applicability, GOEIC import-conformity or inspection route, IEC 62109 safety evidence, EgyptERA/EETC or distribution-company grid-code acceptance, net-metering or tariff-program documents where applicable, 50 Hz settings, EMC evidence, and a China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 crosswalk.
