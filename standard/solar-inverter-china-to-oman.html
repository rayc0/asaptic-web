Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Oman, do not rely only on China GB/T reports. The practical package should confirm DGSM / MoCIIP standards applicability, IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence, the OPWP / grid-company or Nama distribution connection route, Sahim rooftop requirements where applicable, 50 Hz country settings, and a China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 crosswalk.
