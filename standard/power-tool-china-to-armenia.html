Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Armenia, the practical mandatory baseline is EAEU and Customs Union conformity with EAC marking: TR CU 004/2011 for low-voltage electrical safety, TR CU 020/2011 for EMC, TR EAEU 037/2016 for RoHS-style restricted substances, and applicable accredited declaration or certification routes; GOST IEC 60745 or 62841 and GOST IEC 62133 battery evidence commonly support the file but do not replace the EAC procedure.
