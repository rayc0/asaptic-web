Exporting Wireless Devices from China to Bangladesh — BTRC Type Approval & BSTI Compliance Guide

Regulatory requirements for exporting wireless and radio telecommunication devices from China to Bangladesh, covering BTRC type approval, BSTI EMC and safety standards, importer registration, and cybersecurity obligations.

Short answer

Wireless devices exported from China to Bangladesh require mandatory BTRC type approval under the Telecommunication Act 2001 before import or sale. A licensed Bangladeshi importer or authorized dealer must be appointed. BSTI certification may be required for EMC and electrical safety. CE/FCC test reports from accredited labs are accepted as supporting evidence but do not replace BTRC approval.
