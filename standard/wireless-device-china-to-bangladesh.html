Public-interest, source-linked comparison of common China Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and cellular IoT device documentation against Bangladesh BTRC mandatory type approval (Bangladesh Telecommunication Act 2001), BSTI/BDS EMC and electrical safety standards (IEC-aligned), BTRC IMEI registration for mobile devices, local importer/agent requirements, and applicable import duty and VAT obligations under NBR.

Short answer

BTRC mandatory type approval (Bangladesh Telecommunication Act 2001) for all radio/telecom terminal equipment, BSTI/BDS EMC emission and immunity standards (IEC/CISPR-aligned), BSTI/BDS electrical safety (IEC 62368-1-aligned, 220 V/50 Hz), BTRC IMEI registration for mobile devices, and local importer/agent requirements are the major Bangladesh compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC/CCC/GB documentation. CCC, SRRC, FCC and CE marks are not recognised by BTRC; separate Bangladesh type approval with test reports from BTRC-accepted overseas accredited laboratories is required.
