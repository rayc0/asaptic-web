Short answer

For BESS exported from China to South Africa, do not assume the whole integrated system needs NRCS LOA unless a compulsory specification clearly captures that product or component. The practical gaps are confirming NRCS scope for regulated electrical components, mapping battery evidence to SANS / IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 expectations, preparing UN 38.3 transport documents, satisfying NRS 097 and municipal embedded-generation rules for grid connection, and providing installation and fire-safety evidence for residential and C&I projects.
