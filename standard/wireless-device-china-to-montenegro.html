Short answer

EKIP mandatory type approval / market notification (CE DoC alone is not sufficient — EKIP notification is a separate obligation), EN harmonised standards via IEST for radio and EMC testing (accepted as technical evidence but EKIP notification still required separately), EN IEC 62368-1 electrical safety for 230 V / 50 Hz / Type C/F, appointment of a local Montenegrin importer / authorised representative, Montenegrin/Serbian language labelling, and cybersecurity per EKIP/NIS framework are the major compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC radio type approval, CCC certification, and GB standards. CCC is not recognised in Montenegro.
