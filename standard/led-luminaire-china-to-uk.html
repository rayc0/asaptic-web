LED Luminaire: China to United Kingdom (UKCA) Compliance

Beta · data under verification — informational only, not a certification conclusion. Regulatory gap analysis for LED luminaires exported from China to the United Kingdom, covering UKCA marking, electrical safety, photobiological safety, ecodesign, EMC, and RoHS requirements.

Dataset 2026-06-11 Last verified 2026-06-12 10 rows
Short answer

LED luminaires exported from China to the UK require UKCA marking (or CE under current transitional easement), compliance with BS EN 60598, ecodesign efficacy thresholds under SI 2021/1095, and a UK Responsible Person — the biggest gap versus China is the CCC-to-UKCA pathway switch and the CE easement end-date uncertainty.
