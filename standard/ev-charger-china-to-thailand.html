Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Thailand, the main gaps are TISI / IEC 61851 technical alignment, connector redesign from GB/T 20234 to the IEC Type 2 / CCS2 ecosystem where specified, MEA or PEA utility connection review, charging-station policy and licensing context, and safety / EMC evidence. Do not assume a blanket mandatory whole-unit TISI certificate without checking the exact charger type, rating, installation route, and latest TISI compulsory-standard list.
