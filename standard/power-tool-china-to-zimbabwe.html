Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Zimbabwe, the baseline is SAZ/ZWS standards that adopt IEC 62841 (with SANS alignment), national conformity and import inspection, and POTRAZ type-approval for cordless tools with radio functions; the grid is 230 V, 50 Hz, so the 50 Hz frequency matches China but the voltage differs from China 220/380 V. Zimbabwe has no EU-style outdoor-noise product marking regime, so noise is handled as occupational and adopted-standard guidance rather than a separate product mark.
