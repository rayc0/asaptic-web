Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters to Cambodia should align with the Institute of Standards of Cambodia (ISC) conformity and import-inspection regime, build evidence to CS/IEC 62133 cell safety, obtain TRC type approval for any wireless function, complete UN 38.3 transport testing, and appoint an in-country importer of record. Cambodia largely adopts IEC and ASEAN standards and has no EU-style battery, RoHS, or producer-responsibility regime, so Chinese GB 31241 testing supports engineering analysis but is not by itself an ISC market-access approval.
