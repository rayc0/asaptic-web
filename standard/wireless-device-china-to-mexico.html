Short answer

For a China-made wireless or IoT device entering Mexico, the hard gaps are IFT homologacion under the LFTR (mandatory for all radio and telecom terminal equipment; FCC and CE authorisations are not recognised), compliance with applicable NOM standards for EMC and electrical safety, designation of a Mexican importer or distributor (required for IFT registration), RFC taxpayer registration for the importer, and Spanish-language product labelling displaying the IFT homologation number. Chinese SRRC, CCC, and domestic labels do not substitute for these Mexican obligations.
