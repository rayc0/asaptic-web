Short answer

North Macedonia has transposed EU RED (2014/53/EU), so CE marking with a Declaration of Conformity (DoC) submitted to AEK is mandatory for wireless devices — no separate national radio type approval is required beyond the CE/DoC route. EMC must comply with EN/CISPR harmonised standards; electrical safety requires EN IEC 62368-1 for 230 V / 50 Hz / Type C/F. An EU Authorised Representative or a North Macedonia-based importer must submit the DoC to AEK and maintain the technical file. Chinese SRRC, CCC, and FCC certifications are not recognised. Macedonian language labelling is required, and GDPR-aligned data-protection obligations apply to connected devices.
