Short answer

Bulgaria is a full EU member state: CE marking under RED 2014/53/EU is mandatory, enforced nationally by CRC (Communications Regulation Commission). Radio performance (EN 300 328 / EN 301 893), EMC (EN 301 489), electrical safety (EN IEC 62368-1:2020+A11), and cybersecurity under RED Art. 3.3(d)-(f) mandatory from 1 August 2025 (EN 18031) are the major gaps versus Chinese SRRC and CCC. An EU Authorised Representative, Bulgarian-language (Cyrillic) labelling, RoHS 2, and WEEE registration with ECOBULPACK or ELITEX are additional hard requirements with no Chinese equivalent. CCC and SRRC approvals are not recognised in Bulgaria.
