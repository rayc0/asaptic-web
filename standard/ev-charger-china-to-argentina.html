Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Argentina, China GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 evidence is useful but not automatically equivalent to Argentina-side acceptance. The main gaps are IEC 61851 / IEC 62196 or IRAM-adopted references requested by buyers or engineers, connector interoperability, ENRE or distributor grid-connection review, 50 Hz configuration, and the fact that Argentina EV policy signals do not by themselves create a single national charger type-approval route.
