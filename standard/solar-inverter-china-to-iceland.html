Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Iceland, the practical gaps are EEA-aligned CE scope screening for LVD, EMC, and RED, EN/IEC safety evidence expected for inverters, Landsnet and Orkustofnun interconnection context, voltage/frequency profile, anti-islanding and protection settings, and ensuring that China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 evidence is treated as baseline only.
