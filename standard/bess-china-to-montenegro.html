Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Montenegro, the practical baseline is a CE-oriented electrical and EMC product-safety route where ISME and project-owner expectations are applied in a EU-candidate context. The key evidence is EN/IEC safety references such as IEC 62619 and IEC 62933, UN 38.3 for lithium transport documentation, and a CGES/REGAGEN-reviewed interconnection and licensing pathway for storage and grid participation.
