Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Serbia, the main gaps are CE-aligned conformity for applicable low-voltage, EMC, and radio functions, Serbian adoption or use of EN/IEC evidence such as TS EN 62109, AERS and EMS project grid-connection approval, anti-islanding and protection settings, and confirmation that China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 evidence is only a technical baseline, not Serbian market authorization.
