Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Azerbaijan, China GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 evidence is useful technical documentation but does not by itself close Azerbaijan market-access gaps. Azerbaijan is not a member of the EAEU, so EAC certification does not by itself satisfy Azerbaijan conformity requirements — the domestic route is AZSTAND (Azstandart) mandatory conformity certification. The main checks are AZSTAND conformity, applicable AZS / GOST / IEC 61851 safety evidence, IEC 61000-series EMC evidence, connector compatibility (IEC 62196 Type 2 AC and CCS2 DC are the international direction — GB/T 20234 connectors are physically incompatible and require hardware change), 50 Hz and 220/380 V grid suitability, and project-specific Azerishig or Azerenerji grid-interface coordination for higher-power installations.
