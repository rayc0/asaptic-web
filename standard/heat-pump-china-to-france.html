Short answer

For France, the key compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps are: CE marking under LVD, EMC Directive, PED, and Ecodesign (EU) 2016/2281; French F-gas attestation de capacité for installers; RE2020 building code integration; and ADEME MaPrimeRénov' SCOP eligibility thresholds. France is an EU member state, so all EU CE marking obligations apply in full.
