Short answer

Morocco requires an IMANOR NM route for occupational filtering respirators, backed by Ministry of Industry import-conformity controls and in-country importer clearance at Casablanca or Tanger Med. Chinese GB/GBT routes and CCC marks are domestic evidence only. For models with radio or communication functions, ANRT agrément is a separate trigger. Morocco does not have EU-style mandatory cross regimes for PPE through RoHS, battery regulation, or outdoor-noise marking in this lane.
