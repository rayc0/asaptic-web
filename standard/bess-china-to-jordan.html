Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Jordan, public sources do not show one universal BESS whole-product certificate. The practical gap is normally a route check across JSMO standards and conformity documents, IEC 62619 / IEC 62933 evidence requested by project stakeholders, UN 38.3 lithium-battery transport files, EMRC / NEPCO grid-connection review, fire and site-safety review, and 50 Hz site settings.
