Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Qatar, the key practical gaps are: (1) verifying whether the inverter falls within QGOSM's current regulated product list requiring a pre-shipment Certificate of Conformity; (2) IEC 62109-2 safety type-test evidence as explicitly required by Kahramaa's Technical Specifications for solar PV grid connection; (3) IEC 62116 anti-islanding compliance; (4) IEC 62920 EMC evidence; and (5) firmware validation against Kahramaa's 50 Hz / 240 V / 415 V grid parameters. China GB/T 37408 and GB/T 19964 baselines do not satisfy Kahramaa's IEC-referenced grid-connection requirements and cannot be substituted without supplementary IEC test evidence.
