Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to FSM, the key practical gap is voltage and frequency: FSM is a 120/240 V, 60 Hz market versus China’s 220/380 V, 50 Hz system. Exporters should revalidate firmware, anti-islanding, ride-through, reconnection, protection thresholds, and harmonics for FSM operation. In practice, the target technical direction is IEEE 1547 and UL 1741 in US-aligned installations, while IEC 62109 remains the baseline electrical safety framework. DRD and state utility requirements should be confirmed per project because FSM regulation is state-based and still maturing.
