Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Tanzania, the practical gaps are TBS PVoC Certificate of Conformity for regulated imports, model-level safety evidence typically mapped to IEC 62109-1 and IEC 62109-2 or accepted Tanzania standards, EWURA licensing or registration for electricity activities where the project is in scope, TANESCO grid-connection acceptance for parallel operation, and Tanzania 50 Hz grid settings. China GB/T evidence is useful technical support but does not replace Tanzania import clearance or project/grid approval.
