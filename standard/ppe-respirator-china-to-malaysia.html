Short answer

For Malaysia, Chinese CNGB/CCC respirator files are a useful technical baseline but are not sufficient for local market placement. Main gate points are DOSH-aligned PPE safety categorization, importer responsibility in-country, and ST/SIRIM pathways for electrical or marked components when applicable. Volts for appliances are 240 V and frequency is 50 Hz in Malaysia, so exporter test data and labeling on electrical accessories should reflect local usage conditions.
