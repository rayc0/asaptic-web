Short answer

MPT radio type-approval is the primary mandatory requirement for wireless devices entering Myanmar. Electrical safety follows MS/IEC standards (230 V, 50 Hz grid). EMC requirements exist but are largely voluntary. Myanmar has no formal horizontal cybersecurity framework for wireless devices. MIC import permits may be required for restricted product categories. Chinese SRRC and CCC documentation does not substitute for Myanmar approvals.

CCC is administered by CNCA (Certification and Accreditation Administration) and requires third-party testing by a designated CB. China's grid is 220 V nominal, 50 Hz.
