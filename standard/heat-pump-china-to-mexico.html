Short answer

China-market air-source heat pump files do not transfer automatically to Mexico. The main gaps are NOM-011-ENER or NOM-023-ENER efficiency testing for applicable air-conditioning heat-pump configurations, NOM-003-SCFI and related electrical-safety review, a Mexican NOM certificate through an approved body such as NYCE or ANCE where applicable, SEMARNAT review for controlled refrigerants, and Spanish labelling and manuals under NOM-024-SCFI and the applicable energy label.
