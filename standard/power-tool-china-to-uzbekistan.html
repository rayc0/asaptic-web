Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Uzbekistan, treat the route as national Uzbekistan conformity assessment under O'zDST and adopted GOST IEC safety and EMC standards, not as an EAEU TR CU or TR EAEU route. Uzbekistan is an EAEU observer only; TR CU/TR EAEU obligations, including horizontal EAEU RoHS TR EAEU 037/2016, do not apply as market law in Uzbekistan.
