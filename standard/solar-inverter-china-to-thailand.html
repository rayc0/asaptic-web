Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Thailand, the main compliance gaps are not only factory test reports. Exporters should check whether any TISI / TIS product-standard route applies to the exact product, prepare IEC 62109 safety evidence, support MEA or PEA project-specific grid-connection review, align inverter settings to Thailand's 50 Hz distribution network, provide EMC evidence, and avoid treating China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 documents as automatic Thai approval.
