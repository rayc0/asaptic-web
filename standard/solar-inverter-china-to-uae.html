Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to the UAE, the practical gaps are MoIAT/ESMA conformity-route screening, DEWA Shams Dubai equipment eligibility where the inverter will be used in Dubai grid-connected PV, IEC 62109-1/-2 safety evidence, anti-islanding and grid-interface evidence under DEWA DRRG requirements, GSO/UAE standard adoption checks, EMC evidence, and re-validation of 50 Hz 230/400 V grid settings against UAE utility parameters rather than relying only on China GB/T or NB/T files.
