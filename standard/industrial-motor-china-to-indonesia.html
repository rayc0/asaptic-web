Short answer

Indonesia's practical gaps are procedural rather than electrical: in-scope three-phase AC motors need Indonesian SNI certification, Pertek/import clearance support, SNI marking, and ESDM energy labelling, while 380 V 50 Hz is aligned with China and the IE-class basis is aligned to IEC 60034-30-1.
