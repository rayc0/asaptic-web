Short answer

Chinese lithium batteries and power banks entering Azerbaijan generally require local importer-led AZSTAND compliance handling, AZS or AZS-adopted IEC 62133 safety evidence, UN 38.3 transport documentation, and in-country importer readiness at Baku. Chinese GB 31241 and CCC are useful baseline inputs but are not alone sufficient for Azerbaijani market access.
