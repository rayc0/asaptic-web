Short answer

Morocco's IMANOR conformity framework (CE DoC broadly accepted under EU Advanced Status), NM IEC 60335-2-40 electrical safety alignment, PNEE energy efficiency labelling direction under ANRE, HCFC refrigerant phasedown under the Montreal Protocol, and voltage adaptation from CN 220 V to Morocco 230 V single-phase are the principal compliance considerations for Chinese air-source heat pumps entering Morocco. CCC is not recognised; CE certification is the practical entry pathway. Frequency is 50 Hz in both countries — no conversion required.
