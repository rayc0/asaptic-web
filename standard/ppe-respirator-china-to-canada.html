Short answer

For Canada, a Chinese KN95/GB 2626 report is not enough for occupational respirator claims. Canadian workplace programs commonly require NIOSH-approved respirators under 42 CFR Part 84 and selection/use/care under CSA Z94.4. Medical or surgical masks, and N95 respirators sold for medical use, are medical devices regulated by Health Canada under the Medical Devices Regulations SOR/98-282; the product route may require an MDL and the Canadian importer/distributor generally needs an MDEL. Canada also requires English/French bilingual labelling.
