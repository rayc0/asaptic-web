Short answer

For refrigerators, Malaysia is typically controlled by the ST-SIRIM route rather than Chinese CCC transfer, with mandatory alignment to 240 V, 50 Hz documentation, MS IEC safety baseline, MEPS/star labelling, and in-country importer handling through destination documentation. R-600a can still be used where tested design limits allow it, but source claims must match Malaysia scope.
