Short answer

For China-made power tools entering Laos, the practical baseline is DOSM conformity certification if the product is on the regulated-goods list, IEC 60745 or IEC 62841 style safety evidence where adopted or accepted, 230 V 50 Hz suitability for the Lao grid, wireless approval for connected tools, and honest documentation that Laos does not have an EU-style horizontal RoHS regime for ordinary power tools.
