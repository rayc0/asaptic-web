Short answer

Chinese household refrigerators entering Peru need target-market validation on several fronts: INACAL-adopted NTP safety and technical requirements, MINEM energy-labeling obligations where applicable, EMC and radio compliance where relevant, MTPE-aligned safety context, and a Peru-established importer of record handling Customs clearance at Callao. China CCC and GB 12021.2/GB 4706.13 are mandatory domestically but are not sufficient for Peru clearance. Peru does not apply EU-style RoHS, EU-style battery EPR, or an EU-style outdoor noise gate for standard refrigeration products.
