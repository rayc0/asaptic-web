Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Montenegro, the practical gates are CE-oriented conformity for in-scope low-voltage and EMC requirements, IEC 61851 evidence, connector compatibility around IEC 62196 Type 2 and CCS2, CGES and REGAGEN project-connection governance, OCPP interoperability where required, and customs clearance and routing documentation for import logistics through Bar.
