Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Kazakhstan, China GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 evidence is useful technical documentation but does not by itself close Kazakhstan market-access gaps. The main checks are EAEU TR CU 004/2011 low-voltage safety, TR CU 020/2011 EMC, applicable GOST / ST RK / IEC 61851 evidence, connector compatibility with IEC 62196 expectations where specified, 50 Hz suitability, and project-specific utility or KEGOC-related grid review for higher-power installations.
