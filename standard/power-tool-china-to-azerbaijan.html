Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Azerbaijan, the practical baseline is national AZSTAND or AzStandard conformity certification for regulated goods against applicable AZS or GOST-adopted IEC 60745 or IEC 62841 safety standards, EMC evidence, national-language labelling, and radio approval where a tool includes a radio module. Azerbaijan is not in the EAEU, so TR CU or TR EAEU documents and EU-style RoHS declarations should not be treated as the target-market route.
