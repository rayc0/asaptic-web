Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers face four major Philippine compliance gaps: (1) electrical safety re-testing to PNS IEC 60335-2-24 — China CCC/GB 4706.13 certification is not accepted by BPS; (2) BPS Import Commodity Clearance (ICC) must be secured through a Philippine importer before customs release — there is no CCC reciprocity; (3) DOE energy testing against Philippine MEPS plus the DOE Energy Label, recalculated from China GB 12021.2 grades which do not transfer; and (4) the 230 V 60 Hz Philippine grid differs from China 220/380 V 50 Hz, so motor, compressor, and rating-plate verification is required. R-600a refrigerant is accepted, but charge documentation against PNS IEC 60335-2-24 flammable-refrigerant provisions must be verified.
