Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Chile, do not assume that a China GB/T test report or GB/T connector makes the charger ready for Chile. SEC product certification is scope-dependent and should be checked against Chile's current product protocol lists; installed charging infrastructure also needs Chilean electrical-installation compliance, grid acceptance where applicable, and IEC 61851 / IEC 62196 alignment instead of a China-only GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 design.
