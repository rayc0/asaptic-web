Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Kazakhstan, the main baseline is EAEU conformity for applicable low-voltage equipment and EMC requirements, usually under TR CU 004/2011 and TR CU 020/2011 with EAC marking where the product is in scope. Project delivery then depends on IEC 62109 / ST RK / GOST evidence, KEGOC or local network grid-connection review, 50 Hz settings, and renewable-energy auction or PPA documentation where the inverter is part of a utility-scale RES project.
