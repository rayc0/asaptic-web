Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Serbia, the practical baseline is CE/EN-oriented product compliance where the equipment falls under low-voltage, EMC, machinery, radio, RoHS, or related product rules; ISS-tracked TS EN IEC 62619 and IEC 62933 evidence is commonly used for battery and system safety; UN 38.3 is needed for lithium-battery transport; and AERS plus EMS grid-operator review is separate for storage projects and interconnection.
