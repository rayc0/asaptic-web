Public-interest, source-linked comparison of China air-source heat pump documentation against Malaysia's SIRIM mandatory product certification (MS/IEC 60335-2-40), Suruhanjaya Tenaga (ST) mandatory energy label and MEPS requirements, MESTECC/DOE refrigerant and ODS controls, and related market obligations.

Short answer

SIRIM mandatory product certification (MS 1062 / IEC 60335-2-40 safety), ST mandatory energy label and MEPS registration (minimum EER), DOE/MESTECC ODS and refrigerant licensing (R-22 import restrictions, R-32 preferred), and voltage adaptation from CN 220 V to MY 240 V are the major Malaysia compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps. CCC is not recognised.
