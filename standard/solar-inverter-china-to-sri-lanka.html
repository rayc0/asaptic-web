Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Sri Lanka, IEC 62109 safety compliance is the primary technical baseline expected by SLSI and project stakeholders. PUCSL and CEB impose grid-connection and net-metering/net-accounting requirements covering anti-islanding, power quality, 50 Hz grid parameters, and protection relay settings. Chinese GB/T inverter approvals do not automatically satisfy Sri Lanka requirements; importers typically need to demonstrate IEC 62109 test evidence and PUCSL/CEB grid-code conformance.
