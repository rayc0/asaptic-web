Short answer

For India, the main gaps are BIS market-access checks for AC-type products and components, IS 1391/IS 302 electrical-safety evidence where applicable, BEE star labelling for covered air-conditioner categories, refrigerant controls under India's ODS rules and Kigali implementation, and a watch item for heat-pump-specific MEPS that are still evolving.
