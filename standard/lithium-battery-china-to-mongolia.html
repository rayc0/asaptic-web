Short answer

For battery and power bank exports from China to Mongolia, GB 31241 and CCC are China-side technical documents only. Mongolia requires MASM-oriented national obligations, MNS safety alignment, CRC verification for radio features where relevant, UN 38.3 transport proof, and use of an in-country importer for market entry.
