Short answer

Wireless devices entering Azerbaijan require ICTA type approval (conformity certificate/registration) for all radio and telecommunications terminal equipment under the Law on Telecommunications (2005). EMC and radio equipment conformity against AzStandard (AZS) national standards — many adopted from GOST, IEC, and ETSI — is required separately. Electrical safety testing against AZS standards (aligned with IEC 62368-1) is needed for the 220 V/50 Hz European-plug (Type C/F) grid. A local authorised importer or distributor registered in Azerbaijan is mandatory. Azerbaijan is not an EAEU member and has its own national regulatory system; Chinese CCC, SRRC, and CE documentation are not recognised and do not substitute for ICTA approvals. Cybersecurity obligations under CERT.AZ and the Law on Information Security apply to connected devices.
