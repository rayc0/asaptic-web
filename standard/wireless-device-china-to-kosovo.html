Short answer

Kosovo transposes EU RED 2014/53/EU through RAEPC: CE marking with a Declaration of Conformity referencing EN/ETSI harmonised standards (EN 300 328, EN 301 893, EN 55032, EN 55035, EN IEC 62368-1, ETSI EN 303 645) is mandatory — no separate national radio certificate is needed. An EU Authorised Representative or Kosovo-based importer must be appointed. SRRC, CCC, and GB standards are not accepted substitutes. Voluntary reference, not legal advice.
