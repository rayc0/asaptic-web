Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to North Macedonia, the main gaps are CE-aligned conformity for applicable low-voltage, EMC, and radio functions, EN/IEC safety evidence such as EN 62109, ERC / MEPSO project grid-connection approval, anti-islanding and protection settings, and confirmation that China GB/T 37408 / GB/T 19964 evidence is only a technical baseline, not North Macedonia market authorization.
