Short answer

Chinese exporters to Mozambique should prepare INNOQ-backed conformity for regulated lithium batteries and power banks, INCM type approval for wireless functions, UN 38.3 transport documentation, and an in-country importer. Mozambique adopts IEC-based NM references for battery safety, does not run an EU-style horizontal battery regime, and Chinese GB/CCC documentation is not automatically sufficient for Mozambique market entry.
