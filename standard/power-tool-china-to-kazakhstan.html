Short answer

For China-made corded or cordless power tools entering Kazakhstan, the mandatory baseline is EAEU or Customs Union conformity under TR CU 004/2011 for low-voltage electrical safety, TR CU 020/2011 for EMC, TR EAEU 037/2016 for RoHS-style hazardous-substance restrictions, and EAC marking through accredited declaration or certification; GOST IEC 60745 or 62841 and GOST IEC 62133 for cells are common technical routes.
