Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Tunisia, the public official sources reviewed did not identify one Tunisia-only mandatory product certificate specific to stationary lithium BESS. The practical gap is usually a project and import package: confirm INNORPI / customs treatment by HS code, provide IEC 62619:2022 and relevant IEC 62933 evidence where required by tender, authority, insurer, lender, or owner, obtain UN 38.3 transport evidence for lithium batteries, and close STEG / ANME grid, 50 Hz, and fire-safety requirements for the installation.
