Short answer

The main India gaps for a China-origin wireless/IoT device are WPC Equipment Type Approval (ETA) for de-licensed radio modules, WPC/SACFA clearance where licensed spectrum or fixed radio sites are involved, BIS CRS registration for notified electronics tested to IS 13252 (Part 1), TEC/MTCTE certification for notified telecom equipment, and import routing through WPC ETA or applicable WPC import permissions. Chinese SRRC and CCC documents do not substitute for Indian approvals.

SRRC is a mandatory China radio approval and is based on Chinese frequency allocations, power limits, and test rules.
