Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Tanzania, first confirm whether each BESS component is regulated by TBS PVoC / CoC or destination inspection, then confirm the IEC 62619 / IEC 62933 evidence requested by TBS, the importer, EWURA, the utility, lender, insurer, or project owner. UN 38.3 transport evidence is separate. EWURA licensing or registration and grid review may apply to storage projects depending on capacity, activity, ownership, and grid connection.
