Short answer

PPE respirators exported from China to Laos require DOSM (Department of Standards and Metrology) mandatory conformity certification for regulated goods. Laos applies GOST/IEC-based MS (Lao National Standards) as reference standards. Chinese KN95 (GB 2626) test reports alone are not sufficient — a DOSM certification or recognised third-party conformity assessment is required. Laos has no horizontal RoHS regime; hazardous substance restrictions do not apply on the same basis as in the EU. National-language (Lao-script) labelling is required for goods sold in the Lao market. Transit is typically via Thailand or Vietnam.
