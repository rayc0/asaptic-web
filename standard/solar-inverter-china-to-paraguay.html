Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Paraguay, the key practical gaps are: (1) confirming INTN recognition or acceptance of NP/IEC evidence for the specific inverter model; (2) obtaining ANDE technical approval and connection conditions for the project; (3) IEC 62109-1/-2 safety type-test evidence; (4) IEC 62116 anti-islanding and IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence; (5) IEC 62920 / IEC 61000 EMC evidence; and (6) firmware validation for Paraguay's 50 Hz, 220/380 V grid. China GB/T 37408-2021, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964-2024 baselines are useful engineering references but should not be treated as automatically accepted substitutes for INTN conformity or ANDE grid-connection review.
