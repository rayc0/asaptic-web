Short answer

ARTCI mandatory homologation (homologation certificate for all radio/telecom terminal equipment) under Loi n°2012-293 relative aux télécommunications, CODINORM technical standards compliance (IEC/ETSI-aligned CI standards), electrical safety compliance (220 V / 50 Hz, Type C/E French-style plug), French-language labelling, engagement of a local Ivorian importer or agent, and DGDDI customs processing are the major Ivory Coast compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC, MIIT network access licence, CCC, and GB-standard documentation. CE and FCC marks are not recognised by ARTCI. ECOWAS harmonisation is in progress but ARTCI homologation remains nationally enforced. The Agence Nationale de la Sécurité des Systèmes d'Information (ANSCI) issues cybersecurity guidelines but mandatory IoT device certification is not yet in force.
