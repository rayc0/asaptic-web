Short answer

For power tools, Malaysia entry is commonly driven by ST-coordinated CoA control through SIRIM QAS with Malaysia-resident importer responsibility, 240 V and 50 Hz suitability evidence, and MCMC handling for wireless or cordless functions where present. There is no EU-style mandatory outdoor-noise power product marking in this lane, and China GB/T evidence still needs Malaysia destination mapping before release.
