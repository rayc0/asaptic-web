Short answer

For BESS exported from China to Indonesia, the safest working assumption is not a single whole-unit certification path. Map the product to adopted SNI battery standards such as SNI IEC 62619 where applicable, prepare IEC 62933-style system safety evidence for project review, keep UN 38.3 transport documentation for lithium batteries, check K3L marking or registration scope, satisfy PLN interconnection and ESDM project rules, and treat TKDN as mandatory mainly where Indonesian procurement, tariff, incentive, or public-project rules require local-content evidence.
