Short answer

Chinese exporters should treat CODINORM/VoC as target-market rules, not only the Chinese GB/GB-T or CCC baseline. For lithium batteries and power banks, the key non-equivalent elements are VoC import conformity, French-language documentation to clear customs at Abidjan or San-Pedro, local importer coordination, ARTCI checks for wireless variants, and in-country reporting workflows. Energy labels and product-level details should be aligned to Côte d'Ivoire market handling even where CN baseline documents already exist.
