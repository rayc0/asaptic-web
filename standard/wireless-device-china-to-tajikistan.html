Short answer

Wireless devices entering Tajikistan require SCCT radio type approval and a frequency permit (for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular, and other radio-frequency products) plus a Tajikstandart Certificate of Conformity issued by an accredited body under the GOST-TR framework. Tajikistan is NOT a member of the EAEU — EAC marking under TR EAEU 037/2016 is not applicable, and a separate Tajik conformity certificate is mandatory. The CIS Mutual Recognition Agreement on conformity assessment partially applies but does not eliminate the need for Tajik-specific documentation. Electrical safety testing must be compatible with the 220-230 V / 50 Hz Type C/F grid. A locally registered importer or authorized representative is required. Chinese CCC, SRRC, and FCC documentation are not recognized and do not substitute for SCCT or Tajikstandart approvals. Russian-language technical documentation is generally accepted.
