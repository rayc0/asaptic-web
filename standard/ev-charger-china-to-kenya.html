Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Kenya, the main practical gaps are KEBS PVoC import conformity where the goods are in scope, proof against applicable Kenyan Standards or approved specifications, IEC 61851 charger-safety evidence where requested, connector interoperability under IEC 62196 or buyer / fleet requirements, EPRA and utility review for electricity supply, metering, tariffs, and high-load sites, and clear mapping from China GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 evidence. A blanket mandatory whole-unit EV charger certificate was not confirmed from official sources; scope should be verified for the exact HS code, model, charger type, and installation route.
