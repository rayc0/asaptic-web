Short answer

For EV chargers exported from China to Tunisia, GB/T 18487 and GB/T 20234 evidence is useful but should not be presented as automatically equivalent to Tunisia acceptance. The practical gaps are INNORPI or buyer use of IEC 61851 / IEC 62196 references, connector interoperability, STEG connection and installation review, 50 Hz configuration, and the fact that Tunisia EV policy incentives do not by themselves create a single charger type-approval route.
