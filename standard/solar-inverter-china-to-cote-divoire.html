Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Côte d'Ivoire, the main gaps are not a single inverter-only approval mark but proof that the model is safe and grid-compatible. IEC 62109-1 and IEC 62109-2 evidence is the practical safety baseline, CODINORM mandatory-status should be checked against the latest compulsory standards list, and any grid-connected project should be cleared through the CIE / CI-ENERGIES / ANARE project route with 50 Hz settings and local interconnection studies. China GB/T evidence is useful technical support but should not be treated as a substitute for Côte d'Ivoire standards, utility approval, or regulatory acceptance.
