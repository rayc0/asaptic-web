Short answer

Chinese household refrigerator manufacturers face five main Bahrain compliance gaps: (1) electrical safety must align with the GSO-adopted IEC 60335-2-24 standard accepted by BSMD (Bahrain Standards and Metrology Directorate under MOIC) — Chinese CCC/GB 4706.13 certification is not automatically accepted and BSMD conformity/registration is required; (2) energy performance must meet the GSO energy-efficiency MEPS and carry the GSO Energy Efficiency Label, with the Chinese 1-to-5 grade not directly transferable; (3) the GSO energy label (Arabic content) must be affixed; (4) the appliance must be supplied through an in-country Bahraini importer/registrant who holds the conformity documentation; and (5) connected (Wi-Fi/Bluetooth) models additionally require TRA type approval. R-600a refrigerant is well-positioned but charge/documentation against the GSO/IEC 60335-2-24 flammable-refrigerant provisions must be verified. Voltage is 230 V / 50 Hz (50 Hz matches China; nominal voltage differs from China's 220 V).
