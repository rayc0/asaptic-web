Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Cote d'Ivoire, do not assume a single blanket charger certificate. The practical gaps are CODINORM or project adoption of IEC 61851, IEC 62196 connector compatibility instead of GB/T 20234, CIE service-connection and load approval, 50 Hz site validation, and separation between EV policy context and technical approval. China GB/T 18487 evidence is useful only as mapped engineering evidence, not as a Cote d'Ivoire approval.
