Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Algeria, the primary compliance pathway involves IANOR NA standards adopting IEC 62109-1 and IEC 62109-2 for inverter safety, CREG regulatory authorisation for power generation projects, Sonelgaz grid-connection technical requirements for the 50 Hz / 230–400 V Algerian grid, IEC 62116 anti-islanding compliance, and IEC 62920 EMC evidence. Algeria's Solar 1000 and subsequent large-scale solar tenders impose project-specific technical specifications and local-content rules. Chinese GB/T 37408-2021 and GB/T 19964-2024 are not automatically accepted as Algeria evidence; supplementary IEC-route test reports and an Algeria-specific Sonelgaz grid settings sheet are required.
