Public-interest, source-linked comparison of common China Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and IoT device documentation against Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC) mandatory type approval under the Nigerian Communications Act 2003, Standards Organisation of Nigeria (SON) conformity assessment (SONCAP), NIS electrical safety standards, local Nigerian importer requirements, and NAFDAC oversight for health-related electronics.

Short answer

NCC mandatory type approval (with NCC sticker on product) under the Nigerian Communications Act 2003, a SONCAP Certificate of Conformity from SON for customs clearance, NIS-based electrical safety compliance (220-240 V / 50 Hz, Type D/G plug), and engagement of a local Nigerian importer are the major Nigeria compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC, MIIT network access licence, CCC, and GB-standard documentation. CE and FCC marks are not recognised by NCC.
