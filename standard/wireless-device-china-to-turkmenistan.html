Short answer

Wireless devices entering Turkmenistan require MINCOM (Ministry of Communications) type approval and frequency permit for all radio and telecom terminal equipment, plus a Türkmenstandart Certificate of Conformity based on GOST-TM standards. Turkmenistan is NOT a member of the EAEU; EAC marking does not apply and separate Turkmen conformity is mandatory. Electrical safety testing is required for the 220 V/50 Hz Type C/F grid. An authorised Turkmen importer entity is required; state import permission may additionally be required for larger shipments. Wi-Fi 5 GHz use requires a separate MINCOM frequency permit. Chinese CCC, SRRC, and CE marks are not recognised and do not substitute for Turkmen approvals. The market is heavily state-controlled (Turkmentelekom infrastructure monopoly) and significant Chinese infrastructure presence means government procurement channels are common.
