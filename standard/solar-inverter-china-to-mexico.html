Short answer

Mexico is not a CE/CCC carry-over market: a China-origin grid-tied PV inverter normally needs NOM electrical-safety coverage through an approved certification body such as NYCE or ANCE where the product NOM applies, installation compliance with NOM-001-SEDE, separate CRE/CFE distributed-generation (GD) interconnection acceptance for projects below the 0.7 MW threshold, and a CONUEE energy-efficiency scope check.
