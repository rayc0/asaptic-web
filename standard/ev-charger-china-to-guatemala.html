Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Guatemala, the practical gates are COGUANOR conformity or adopted-standard evidence where applicable, CNEE and distributor coordination for grid-connected installations, 120/240 V 60 Hz input compatibility, IEC 61851 safety and IEC 61000 EMC evidence, and connector hardware suitable for the local vehicle and charging ecosystem. Because Guatemala is in the Americas electrical and vehicle-import region, SAE J1772 (Type 1) AC and CCS Combo 1 DC are the likely project baseline, but exporters should confirm the required connector with CNEE, EEGSA, Energuate, the charge-point operator, and the tender documents before quoting. China GB/T 20234 connector chargers are physically incompatible without hardware redesign, and GB/T 18487 / GB/T 27930 evidence is not a Guatemala approval substitute.
