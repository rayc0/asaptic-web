Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Botswana, the mandatory baseline is conformity assessment and import inspection under the Botswana Bureau of Standards (BOBS) using BOS standards that adopt IEC and SANS, with BOS/IEC 62841 as the core safety reference and BOCRA type approval for the radio function of cordless tools. There is no Botswana equivalent of the EU outdoor-noise product marking scheme, so noise is handled through general product-safety and standards conformity rather than a dedicated noise-marking law.
