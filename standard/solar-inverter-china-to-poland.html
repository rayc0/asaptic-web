Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Poland, practical gaps are: confirm the applicable PKN route and project-level approval path; validate that IEC 62109-1/-2 safety testing, IEC 62116 anti-islanding, IEC 61727 utility-interface evidence, and IEC 62920 / IEC 61000 EMC documentation are accepted for Poland installations; and prove 230/400 V 50 Hz performance, protection thresholds, reconnection timing, and weather derating for Polish climate conditions. Poland's large and growing PV market means active URE and PSE oversight for larger installations. China GB/T 37408, NB/T 32004, and GB/T 19964 documents are useful but not automatically interchangeable with Poland evidence.
