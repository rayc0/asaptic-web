Short answer

RAK mandatory type approval certificate (not substitutable by CE DoC, CCC, or FCC alone), ATK product conformity certificate for general product safety, EN/ETSI-aligned testing for radio and EMC (BiH aligns as EU candidate), electrical safety to IEC 62368-1 for 230 V / 50 Hz / Type C/F, appointment of a local authorised importer, Bosnian/Croatian/Serbian trilingual labelling, and cybersecurity advisory compliance are the major compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC radio type approval, CCC certification, and GB standards. BiH entity-level governance (Republika Srpska and Federation of BiH) adds jurisdictional complexity. CCC is not recognised in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
