Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Jordan, the mandatory baseline is conformity with applicable JSMO technical regulations and a conformity certificate, with JS standards that adopt IEC/GSO/EN such as IEC 62841 used to show safety; cordless tools with wireless functions also need TRC type approval. Jordan uses 230 V, 50 Hz, so the 50 Hz frequency matches China but the nominal voltage differs from China's 220/380 V.
