Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering FSM, the practical gaps are 120/240 V 60 Hz electrical revalidation against China’s 220/380 V 50 Hz baseline, conversion from GB/T 20234-style interfaces to SAE J1772 for AC and CCS1 for DC, UL-direction EVSE safety evidence such as UL 2594 or UL 2202, FCC Part 15 / IEC 61000 EMC evidence for electronic and networked units, state utility site-connection approval, and typhoon-exposed island installation resilience. FSM has a compact-with-US context and state-based requirements, and the EV market is nascent and largely project-driven.
