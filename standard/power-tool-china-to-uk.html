Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering Great Britain, the mandatory baseline is compliance under the Supply of Machinery (Safety) Regulations 2008, Electrical Equipment (Safety) Regulations 2016 where applicable, EMC Regulations 2016, outdoor-noise rules for listed outdoor equipment, UK RoHS, and battery rules for battery-powered tools; BS EN 62841 is voluntary but commonly used to show conformity.
