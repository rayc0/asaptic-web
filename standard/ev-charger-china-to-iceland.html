Short answer

For China-made EV chargers entering Iceland, the practical gaps are EEA-aligned market placement and CE documentation, EN/IEC 61851 safety and EMC coverage by model, Type 2 / CCS2 connector alignment in the European interface environment, grid interconnection and service-operator route checks, OCPP backend acceptance where required, and site-level residual-current, earthing, and commissioning controls.
