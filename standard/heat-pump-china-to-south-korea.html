Short answer

KC certification under ECPSA, mandatory KEMCO energy-efficiency grading, Top Runner SCOP targets, Korean refrigerant controls under the Fluorine Management Act, and 60 Hz electrical compatibility are the major South Korea compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps. CCC is not recognized, and China 50 Hz compressor designs are not directly compatible with South Korea's 60 Hz grid.
