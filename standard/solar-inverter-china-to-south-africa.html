Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to South Africa, the main gaps are determining whether the model needs an NRCS Letter of Authority under the applicable compulsory specification route, preparing SANS / IEC 62109 safety evidence, proving NRS 097-2-1 utility-interface behavior for Eskom or municipal connection, configuring South Africa 50 Hz settings, and documenting EMC evidence under SANS adoptions of CISPR / IEC standards.
