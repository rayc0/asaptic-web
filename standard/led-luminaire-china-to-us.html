Short answer

NRTL certification (UL 1598 + UL 8750) and FCC Part 15B SDoC are the critical US gaps for Chinese LED luminaires. Note: there is no single federal consumer safety mark in the US — NRTL listing is effectively required via the National Electrical Code (NEC), Authorities Having Jurisdiction (AHJ), major retailer specifications, and OSHA workplace rules, not as one overarching federal mandate. FCC Part 15B SDoC is a genuine federal requirement. ENERGY STAR and DLC QPL listing are voluntary but de facto required for commercial utility rebate eligibility.
