Short answer

South Korea is not a CE/CCC carry-over market: in-scope grid-tied PV inverters need compulsory KC safety certification and KC marking under the Electrical Appliances and Consumer Products Safety Control Act, evidence against applicable KS C/IEC inverter standards, KN EMC compliance where applicable, and separate KEPCO distributed-generation interconnection acceptance.
