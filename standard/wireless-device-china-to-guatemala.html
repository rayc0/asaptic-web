Short answer

The main Guatemala compliance gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are: SIT mandatory homologación under Ley General de Telecomunicaciones (Decreto 94-96) — all radio and telecom terminal equipment requires a SIT homologación certificate before import or sale, with no recognition of CCC or SRRC approvals; FCC and CE test reports may be used as a basis within the SIT technical dossier but do not substitute for the Guatemalan certificate; an authorized Guatemalan importer with NIT/SAT registration is required; COGUANOR NGO electrical safety and EMC compliance (standards harmonised with IEC/ISO/COPANT); product compatibility with Guatemala's 120 V / 60 Hz power system and Type A/B (US-style) plugs; Spanish language labelling; and advisory cybersecurity obligations under Ley de Acceso a la Información Pública with no specific product cybersecurity certification law currently in force.
