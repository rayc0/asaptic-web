Short answer

For China-made electric or cordless power tools entering the EU, the mandatory baseline is CE compliance under machinery law, LVD where applicable, EMC, outdoor noise for listed outdoor equipment, RoHS, and the EU Battery Regulation for battery-powered tools; EN 62841 is voluntary but commonly used to show conformity.
