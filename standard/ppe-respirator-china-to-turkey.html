Short answer

For Turkey, filtering respirators (FFP1 / FFP2 / FFP3 intent) are treated through the EU PPE route transposed into the Turkish market context: Regulation (EU) 2016/425 logic applies via Customs Union alignment, EN 149 is the core technical standard, and Category III conformity requires mandatory Notified Body involvement (Module B and Module C2 or D). CN GB 2626-2019 reports are technical input only and are not a direct substitute for Turkish placement. In-country import, distributor and representative support is required for Turkish market handling, and product logistics commonly route through Mersin and Istanbul Ambarlı.
