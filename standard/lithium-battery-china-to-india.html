Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters to India should treat BIS CRS registration as a separate Indian market-access gate: sealed secondary cells/batteries are covered by IS 16046 / IS 16047 technical benchmarks, power banks are listed under Electronics CRS, UN 38.3 and DGCA dangerous-goods controls apply for transport, and BEE usually requires product-scope screening rather than automatic registration for standalone power banks.
