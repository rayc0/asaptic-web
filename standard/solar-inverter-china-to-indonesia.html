Short answer

For solar PV inverters exported from China to Indonesia, the main gaps are Indonesian SNI / IEC 62109 safety evidence where the product is in a mandatory-SNI scope, ESDM rooftop PLTS rules, PLN 50 Hz grid-connection and anti-islanding settings, TKDN local-content eligibility for covered projects, and K3L registration where the electrical-goods category is in scope.

NB/T 32004		 Indonesia rooftop solar projects connected to the network of a holder of an electricity supply business licence for public interest are governed by the ESDM rooftop PLTS regulation.
