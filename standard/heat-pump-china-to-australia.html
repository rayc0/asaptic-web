Short answer

GEMS registration, MEPS and Zoned Energy Rating Label evidence under the applicable air-conditioner determination, RCM marking through an Australian or New Zealand Responsible Supplier, EESS safety evidence to AS/NZS 60335.2.40, and Ozone/SGG refrigerant controls are the major Australia compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps.
