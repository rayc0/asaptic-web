Short answer

Chinese lithium battery and power bank exporters to Zambia should plan for ZABS conformity assessment and import inspection (with the ZABS mark where the product is regulated), ZS/IEC 62133 cell safety evidence, ZICTA type approval for any wireless function, UN 38.3 transport documentation, and an in-country importer — none of which are automatically satisfied by Chinese GB 31241 or CCC certification. Zambia generally adopts IEC-based standards and does not operate an EU-style horizontal battery, RoHS, or outdoor-noise regime.
