Short answer

Australia does not use a separate toy conformity mark comparable to the European marking route. The main China-to-Australia gaps are compliance with ACCC mandatory safety standards under Australian Consumer Law, small-parts and projectile requirements for toys intended for children up to and including 36 months, Australian warning and labelling practice, and evidence aligned to AS/NZS ISO 8124 rather than only GB 6675 / CCC.
