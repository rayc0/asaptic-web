Short answer

For Chinese inverters exported to Switzerland, the primary practical gap is Switzerland-facing conformity evidence under SNV-adopted CE-aligned rules, Swissgrid interconnection and commissioning requirements, and local voltage-profile configuration, including 230/400 V operation and 50 Hz implementation, while confirming whether Switzerland requires EN 62109 and anti-islanding parameters for the exact model.
