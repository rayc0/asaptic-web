Short answer

NTA mandatory type approval (Telecommunications Act 2053/1997) for all radio and telecom terminal equipment, NBSM/NS EMC emission and immunity standards (CISPR/IEC-aligned), NBSM/NS electrical safety (IEC 62368-1-aligned, 230 V/50 Hz, plug types C/D/M), local importer registration with NTA, and Nepal Customs import duty obligations are the major Nepal compliance gaps compared with Chinese SRRC/CCC/GB documentation. CCC, SRRC, FCC, CE, and BIS marks are not recognised by NTA; a separate Nepal NTA Type Approval Certificate with the NTA approval number on the product label is required before import.
