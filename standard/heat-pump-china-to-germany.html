Short answer

Germany is the EU's largest heat-pump market and applies all EU CE/Ecodesign/F-gas/PED requirements plus national rules: GEG building energy act, ChemKlimaschutzV F-gas handling ordinance, VDI 4645/DIN EN 14511 installation norms, TA Lärm noise limits, and BAFA/KfW BEG subsidy eligibility criteria. Chinese air-source heat pumps must clear EU CE marking, Ecodesign SCOP thresholds, F-gas phase-down restrictions, and these German-specific requirements before market entry.
