China-to-EU Sodium-ion Battery Storage Compliance Gap Matrix

Beta · data under verification — informational only, not a certification conclusion. Public-interest, source-linked comparison of common China sodium-ion battery storage documentation against EU CE, Battery Regulation, transport, safety, and due-diligence expectations.

Short answer

Na-ion faces the same EU Battery Regulation 2023/1542 obligations as Li-ion, including carbon footprint, due diligence, and battery passport requirements, plus CE and EN IEC 62619 expectations; one upside is easier UN 38.3 transport classification for many Na-ion cells.
