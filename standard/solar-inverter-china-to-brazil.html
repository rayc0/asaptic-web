China-to-Brazil Solar Inverter Compliance Gap Matrix

Short answer

INMETRO certification to ABNT NBR 16149/16150 grid and safety requirements is mandatory; Chinese certificates are not accepted.

SGCC / CSG distributed-generation connection procedures (provincial level)		 Beyond INMETRO product certification, connecting a grid-tied PV inverter to the Brazilian grid requires registration of the distributed-generation unit with ANEEL (Agencia Nacional de Energia Eletrica) under Normative Resolution REN 1000/2021 (which consolidated and superseded REN 482/2012 and REN 687/2015). The actual grid connection request and technical approval are processed by the local electricity distributor (concessionária or permissionária), which reviews inverter specifications, issues a connection opinion (parecer de acesso), and permits energisation. Inverters must comply with the technical standards referenced in REN 1000/2021, including ABNT NBR 16149 compatibility and anti-islanding requirements. The distributor may also require adherence to PRODIST (Electrical Energy Distribution Procedures in the National Electric System), Module 3.ANEEL Resolucao Normativa REN 1000/2021 (distributed generation framework)
ANEEL PRODIST Module 3 (technical connection conditions)
ABNT NBR 16149:2013 (anti-islanding and grid-interface requirements, referenced by REN 1000)
Lei 14.300/2022 (Legal Framework for Distributed Micro- and Mini-Generation — Lei do Marco Legal)		 Brazilian distributors require INMETRO-certified inverters that have been verified to meet REN 1000/2021 and PRODIST Module 3 technical conditions. CN grid-company approval records and GB/T anti-islanding parameters do not satisfy Brazilian distributor technical review. The importer or local EPC contractor must submit a connection request dossier (including INMETRO certificate, technical datasheet, single-line diagram) to the relevant concessionária for each project or inverter model. Additionally, import into Brazil requires a Brazilian importer of record registered in SISCOMEX, NCM tariff classification, and payment of applicable II (import duty), IPI, PIS/COFINS, and ICMS taxes.
