Short answer

BSTI mandatory certification (BSTI mark) with pre-shipment inspection for listed electrical goods including imported air conditioners and heat pumps, BDS/IEC 60335-2-40 electrical safety compliance, SREDA/BERC energy-label direction, DoE ODS and refrigerant permit for R-22 phase-down, and BTRC type-approval for wireless-enabled units are the major Bangladesh compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps. Grid compatibility (220 V / 50 Hz) is excellent. CCC is not recognised.
