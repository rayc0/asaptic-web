Public-interest, source-linked comparison of common China Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and IoT device documentation against Ethiopian Communications Authority (ECA) mandatory type approval under Proclamation 1148/2019, Ethiopian Standards Agency (ES) standards for EMC and electrical safety, the 220-230 V/50 Hz Type-C/F plug environment, Ethiopian authorized importer requirements, and advisory alignment with the Computer Crime Proclamation 958/2016 on cybersecurity.

Short answer

The main Ethiopia gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are: mandatory ECA type approval under Proclamation 1148/2019 (CE, FCC, and CCC are not recognised by ECA), ES standards for EMC emissions and immunity based on CISPR frameworks, electrical safety compliance for Ethiopia's 220-230 V/50 Hz Type-C/F plug environment, appointment of a local Ethiopian authorized importer or agent, and advisory awareness of the Computer Crime Proclamation 958/2016 on cybersecurity. Ethiopia's telecom market opened in 2021 and ECA type approval enforcement is active.
