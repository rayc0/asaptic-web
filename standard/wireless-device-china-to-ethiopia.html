Short answer

The main Ethiopia gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are: mandatory ECA type approval certificate under the Communications Service Proclamation No. 1148/2019 (CE, FCC, and CCC are not recognised by ECA), ESA technical standards for EMC aligned with IEC, electrical safety compliance for Ethiopia's 220V/50Hz type-C/E/F plug environment (IEC 62368-1 or equivalent), appointment of a local Ethiopian importer or authorised agent, ERCA customs clearance at import, and IoT data handling awareness under the Cybercrime Proclamation No. 1038/2021.
