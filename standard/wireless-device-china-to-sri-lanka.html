Short answer

TRCSL type approval (mandatory for all radio and telecom terminal equipment under Sri Lanka Telecommunications Act No. 25 of 1991), SLS-aligned EMC compliance, 230 V / 50 Hz Type G electrical safety, designation of a local authorised importer, and awareness of significant import duties (NBT / PAL / CESS) are the major Sri Lanka compliance gaps compared with Chinese MIIT/SRRC type approval, CCC, and GB-standard documentation. CE, FCC, and CCC marks are not recognised by TRCSL.
