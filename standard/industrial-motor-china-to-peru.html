Short answer

The first Peru gap is frequency: industrial motors must be suitable for 60 Hz service, so a China-market 50 Hz motor is not directly compatible. Peru uses NTP IEC 60034-aligned motor standards, INDECOPI conformity routes for listed products, OSINERGMIN grid context, and energy-efficiency labelling expectations; CCC is not recognized as Peru approval.
