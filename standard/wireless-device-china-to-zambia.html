Short answer

The main Zambia gaps for a China-made Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or IoT device are mandatory ZICTA type approval under the ICT Act No. 15 of 2009 (CE and FCC not recognised), ZABS ZS EMC standards compliance (IEC-based), electrical safety alignment for 230 V / 50 Hz Type G (BS 1363) sockets, appointment of a local Zambian authorised importer or dealer, and advisory alignment with the Cyber Security and Cyber Crimes Act 2021. CCC is not recognised in Zambia. SADC harmonised type approval is under development and not yet operative.
