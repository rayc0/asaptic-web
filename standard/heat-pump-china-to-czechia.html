Short answer

CE marking under LVD and EMC directives, F-gas certification under EU 2024/573 and Czech Act No. 73/2012 Sb., Ecodesign SCOP thresholds (EN 14511/EN 14825), Pressure Equipment Directive classification (implemented as NV 2016/208 Sb. in Czechia), and Czech-language documentation requirements are the major compliance gaps for Chinese air-source heat pumps entering the Czech market.
