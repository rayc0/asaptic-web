Short answer

Myanmar standards are largely voluntary, but BESS projects may still need MSTRD conformity evidence where in scope, MIC import approval for large systems, MEPE/ESE grid acceptance, and UN 38.3 dangerous-goods documentation. Myanmar has no horizontal RoHS regime, so EU-style RoHS rows should not be mapped as a local legal duty.
