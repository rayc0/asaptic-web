Short answer

For Canada, the main BESS gaps are SCC-accepted Canadian electrical certification, CSA/UL system and battery safety evidence, provincial fire/building and electrical-code installation approval, CSA C22.3 No. 9 utility interconnection, TDG/UN 38.3 transport documentation, and English/French labels and instructions.

UN 38.3 transport testing		 Lithium cells, modules, racks, and BESS equipment containing lithium batteries are dangerous goods for transport.
